1> What was the role of risk management in the game? What choices did you make and what trade-offs did you confront in terms of knowledge, protection, and insurance? In what ways did preparation for risk affect development (immediately, or after some time)?

1.1>The role of risk management in the game Risk Horizon is to ensure wealth growth while decrease the impact of the comet hits.
1.2> Choices I made:
a. Always tried to set the alarm 1~2 degrees higher than the comet research showed, so that I can survive from hits.
b. Keep building and upgrading my pods during the whole process to ensure I have enough money to pass the level.
c. Chose to buy insurance when more comets appeared.
1.3> Trade-offs
a. Raising the alarm cost time and money, I would usually stop researching the comets to save time to raise the alarm.
b. If the process-bar was green and there was not much time left, I would stop upgrading my pods to prevent spending more money.
c. If the coming comet was highly risky, and my alarm level was much lower than it should be, I would choose to buy an insurance rather than raise the alarm.
1.4> Preparation for risk affect development
a. Immediately
Researching is a time-cost work, but it can help us to identify the accurate risk level of a comet. If the level goes higher while researching, it's time to pull up the alarm bar.
b. After some time
Having an insurance ahead of being hit my a highly risky comet is very helping to save wealth.
