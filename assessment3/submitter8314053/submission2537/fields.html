At the first times I played this game, my strategies are quite stiff and inflexible due to lack of experiences. After many faults, I realized that I had to change to adapt with current situation, I started to concerned and researched on comets such as severity, chance of hits and predict time of collision to had a wise choice among methods. In higher level, the game went harder, concentration was must have due to complicated development and the choice of method should be exact.
