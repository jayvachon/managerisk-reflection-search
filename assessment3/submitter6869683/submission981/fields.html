Risk management played a key role in the game ‘Risk Horizon’. The game illuminated the practical aspect of risk management strategies in the idealistic town with the aim of the objective of balancing the risk preparations and potential development. In order to maintain the balance between the same, several trade offs were faced along with managing the finance for risk management, protection from shocks, insurance and development of the community. The limited financial resources called for their distribution in an economical and efficient way. The powers of research, protection and insurance were the components without which risk could not be managed and ultimately would have resulted in greater loss due to disaster. Difficult trade offs were experienced especially the one between investing in development and in protection. In order to protect the community from the disaster and the following loss, protection was extremely necessary. The insurance helped in reducing the extent of damage caused as the insurance companies provide with aid to repair. Thus these activities involved in risk management did affect the development by reducing its pace. Development of community stagnated for a while when the finance was being used for protection and insurance. However, the development did not show any depleted trend.

I played the game thrice. Loosing the game taught me the importance of development within the time frame. Along with the protection and insurance level, keeping an eye on the development level was very necessary. Thus, by taking this factor in account I accomplished three levels and reached fourth. The game taught the essentials of developing a community with keeping every aspect in mind particularly the risk management for the potential danger that could impart great loss. The game was self explanatory which was its best quality with a good difficulty level that showed the strategies of risk management require both time and money. If tasked with development of new version of the game I would like to add up some more risk management strategies apart from insurance and protection. Migration of the people living in the community to safer areas when the warning is declared – is a component important to risk management. Also, improving the after damage aid will provide a commendable assistance.

Finally, the experience illuminated and taught the significance of risk management in developing and under developed areas.
