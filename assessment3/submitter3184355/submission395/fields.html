1.) I had several opportunities to confront risk in the game. The role of risk management was to identify the risk, research the possibility of potential loss (gain knowledge), build up protection against the consequences and if protection is not adequate, help coping with its consequences (insurance). I had to balance my resources very carefully between development, insurance and protection and also research of risks. At first, I invested in the highest insurance option and built high level of protection in the cost of development. Buying insurance and building up protection took a huge amount of resources from development which hindered future income badly.

However, protection and insurance saved a lot of resources for later development.

Some aspects of the game in terms of risk management (course material week 3) can be identified as follows:

  1. protection of the hubs from the flood is the prevention and preparedness, which should be the most important effort to take, instead of the latter two:
  2. insurance of damage is identified as the reconstruction relief, which is also a very important asset and should not be missed, as there might be no adequate level of protection exists
  3. repairing damaged hubs could be also called as emergency response, which should be minimum, compared to the level of the first two undertakings.

2.) I tried several methods to balance between types of risk management and development. For example: high level insurance with low level protection didn’t pay out very well, as I always received some damage. Researching risks helped to calculate the necessary level of protection, as high chance risks with minimum potential loss did not require costly protection or insurance, which would have taken valuable resources from development. On the other hand, low chance risks with high potential loss could not be taken lightly, as I received huge losses from low chance and high severity risks.

Investing only in development with low level insurance and protection, lead to high damages which I couldn't be mitigated efficiently. Research also didn’t help a lot to avoid losses when I only focused on knowledge briefly. What I learned is protection from risk is the best way to avoid any losses whatsoever but alone is not enough to provide safety. Insurance is also needed to mitigate losses, combined with knowledge of risks to estimate the necessary level of insurance.

At one moment I was very angry at the game, because a very low chance damage actually hit and caused huge losses, which ruined my chances to complete lvl 5. Then I realized, that the game is not unfair or at least not more, than real life itself. I should not underestimate risk, as no matter how low is the chance for damage, it could actually happen. So, even very low chance risks need to be prepared for with adequate level of research, protection and insurance.

3.) I think it would help to numerate future income from development (of hubs). As real life actors have better knowledge of the actual cost and its economical effect of protection and insurance on development and future income, I would like to see what level of income different level of hubs earn. It would help to calculate, whether investment in development or investment in protection and insurance would help more in reaching future goals.

I would also implement differentiation in the level of protection for different hubs. For example, higher protection for lower cost for only several, chosen hubs would help to focus protection on the most important assets (as shelters in real life against floods, tornadoes, etc. to protect the most precious “asset”, human life).
