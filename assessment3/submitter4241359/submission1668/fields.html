The main role of risk management in the game was to take action in the three different ways of tackling risk; research, protection and insurance at the same time that you had to focus in developing your “town”. The choices that I have made were mainly research the different asteroids while I was saving money for developing the houses for gaining higher returns and reinvest the profits in insurance, protection and in keeping developing the houses. Thanks to that, most of the time I had plenty of time; after the asteroid hit the rock, for improve connections among town in order to reduce the damage produced by the hit. The preparation for this hitting allowed me to not have to spend money in fixing damages and keep growing. Due to I could reach level four very easily. All this prevention and protection policy that I followed was translated in growing not immediately but I would say that almost.

I have played more than once and what I did differently was try to develop the houses at the same time I was spending resources in research or in protection, as I said before, that allowed me to gain higher returns and reinvest the pay offs in more protection and progress. Before I was all the time trying to increase the protection and I did not pay that much attention to research, I thought that only with protection and insurance would have been enough. What I have learned is that you cannot be focused just in one task like developing or improving the protection, you have to find a balance within developing, protection, research and insurance. As long you can find that balance your “town” will grow up and better protected and therefore a better and safer place to live.

I am not sure but maybe I would add some different ways of improving the town, not just developing the houses, something like investing in water, energy, healthcare or education in order to see how investing in these areas can improve in many different manners.  As a consequence you could see how different areas develop a city or a country.
