The role of risk management was to protect the pod and community from Metroid. Since there is a limited time and money, trade-offs are being made. In order to gain more knowledge about the risk, time must be spent to observe the Metroid. Time is also needed to develop new constructions and build protection. Obtaining protection is done with investing money. Money is also needed when building and for insurance. Thus managing time and money is important. Preparation for risk will use up resources which slows down development.
