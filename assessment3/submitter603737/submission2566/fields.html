1. The game is designed to test one’s ability to manage risk while working towards development of community. Player has to balance between investing into development activities and managing risk.

Hence risk management plays a crucial role in the game as one cannot go on to win any level without managing the risk posed by the comets.

On a macro level, there is a choice between investing in development or in risk management.On a micro level, risk management in the game can be further classified based on the choices we have for managing risk –

1.Knowledge –  least investment -  however knowledge alone does not help. Player has to use this knowledge in making decision about risk management. 

2. Protection: Investment flexible depending on the risk. The player may purchase protection equalling the severity of risk. However purchasing required protection takes time and in case of high severity comet, player needs to start the purchase of protection well in advance else despite having enough money the player may not be able to protect the place leading to high damages.

3. Insurance: Investment  high.The price of insurance depends on two factors –

(i) Level of Development –higher the development (number/ level of pods) higher the insurance cost.

(ii) Coverage percentage: higher the coverage higher the insurance cost.

Choice of purchasing insurance depends on the kind of impending risk. In case there are several higher severity comets about to hit the place insurance would be a better option, though it is the costlier option.

In what ways did preparation for risk affect development (immediately, or after some time)?

For simplification I’m breaking down this into three cases -

CASE I – No or In-adequate preparation: we can spend all the available resources on building pods resulting into higher development. However after comet strikes there is huge loss and lots of resources are required to get back to same level of development.

Case II – Adequate Preparation: This is the best case scenario where initially development is bit slower than CASE I, since some investment is made in preparation. However after some time, when the comet strikes, development is not affected much. Hence overall development is best in this case.

CASE III – More than required preparation: where more than required investment is made in preparation, leading to lesser investment in development - initially development is slowest leading to lesser wealth creation. But the impact of calamity on development is least.
