I have already imagined how difficult is to the politicians to deal with the trade-offs they confront every day. Thus, it seems that the ability (skills) of politicians and their teams is increasingly important, given that cities are increasing and resources are increasingly constrained. They have to be able to understand how to manage risks in an adequate way to ensure that their objectives shall be achieved. They also have to build communities connections to enhance their capacity to face potencial threats. The knowing phase has to be structured with mecanisms to identify and analyse risks, including their severity and likelihood. And, most important is the ability to identify their nature and complexity. And monitoring emerging risks is also a very important task that has to be done.
