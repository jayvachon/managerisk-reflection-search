Addressing the first questions (right! Questions! With an “S”, because there are three questions combined in there!) To make it clear, my answers are marked (1-1), (2-1) and (3-1). Now for the first question of the first set, (1-1): To my honest opinion the role of Risk Management in the game was sort of like an decision making scale or balance where in the gamer is put into a situation where he/she has to decide and prioritize which course of action to do to ensure growth of his community while at the same time also ensuring security. (3-1): To me this game teaches gamers to manage unforeseen risk or events such as natural calamities and disasters, although in the game it was in a form of comets, in real life it has the same aspects, you can study it to predict not when it will hit but the potential damage it can bring and so gives you time to act early and ahead of it and resulting into either elimination and if not possible then mitigation of the aftermath.

 

Addressing the second question, I played the game more than once and learning from my earlier mistakes, I have learned that the early you prepare for an unforeseen event really goes a long way.
