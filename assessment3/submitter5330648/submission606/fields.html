The strategy of knowledge could provide the information of the degree of adverse effect and occurring opportunity, which is helpful for the player to make a decision about whether the building investment or protection investment. The strategy of protection plays a significant role in the game, which is a method of avoiding the loss in advance. The last strategy of insurance could help the player to recover the loss quickly and less costly when the adverse event happened. On the other hand, all of the three strategies would cost the resource. Especially, the protecting could spend lots of time that could be used to arrive at high level. So based on above advantages and disadvanges of each strategy, the player should allot their resource on them reasonably and efficiently.
