1.     Firstly, the risk in the game is the comet damaging and destroying the pods you built in the city, so the game offers facilities to handle with the risk, beginning with previous knowledge of the risk as a result of the respective number of comets, so doing the research process in the game would be only useful in the beginning before the risk happen as I have experienced during the play. And after we diagnose the risk, we make the arrangements to confront the risk either to pay for a protection or insurance. In this process we should balance between the pods we have built and the insurance or protection we are going to get in order to maintain it. After we purchase the insurance or pay for the protection, we begin to build the pods, regarding that usually we should left a balance for the coping process if the risk taken place. After building the pods to develop the city, the risk of comets will take place, and the damage it causes will vary according to the confrontation and the development processes we have made previously. After the risk happen, it will destroy the pods and damage the development, so here we are going to cope the effects by repairing the pods.
