Risk management is the process of confronting risks,preparing for them and copying with there effects.
There are 2 goals of risk management
  • to build resilience/positive change,ability to mitigate losses for adverse shock and for faster recovery
  • to build prosperity 
 The risk management process include :
  • Identify potential exposure to loss
  •  Measure frequency and severity 
  • Examine alternative 
  • Decide which alternative to use 
  • Implement the chosen technique
  • Monitor results
