Ø  What was the role of risk management in the game? What choices did you make and what trade-offs did you confront in terms of knowledge, protection, and insurance? In what ways did preparation for risk affect development (immediately, or after some time)?

Risk Management played a critical role in identification, analysis and accepting or mitigation of the risk. Certain decisions had to be made as it involved investment. Trade off’s had to made between cost of Insurance, Investment on development, Time to be spent on research and finally cost on preparation for loss mitigation. As I started to play the game, I was concentrating more on High Risk cover paying a huge amount for insurance which led to scarcity of funds for the development. So it was really critical to balance the cost of Insurance and investment on development to build a strong community which was on the Continous path of development and also had Risk insurance in case of any extreme unavoidable scenario to mitigate the losses incurred. Enough attention wasn’t paid to research on critical aspects and impact of asteroids hitting the planet. Their impact, severity wasn’t taken into consideration which was a major risk. To understand how to make a sound decision considering the development and Insurance Cover was the key understanding out of the game.

 

Ø  If you played the game only once, what would you do differently if you were to play it again? Alternatively, if you played the game more than once, what did you do differently after the first time? What were the main things you learned from the game?

As I started to play the game, I was concentrating more on High Risk cover paying a huge amount for insurance which led to scarcity of funds for the development. As I played the game couple of times, understanding was better. To really evaluate the risk and then balance out the investment ensuring optimum amount was spent on the development. It was important to construct a Failure Mode Effect Analysis chart to identify reasons for threat, research of the events, identifying minor/major events and what would it lead to incase the activities completed to mitigate it failed. Critical analysis in case of failure is very important to understand the damage this can cause – be it for the family, community or the country. Careful study/research of the events and pro-active approach is of utmost importance to ensure that we are well prepared in advance. So as I understood the concept better after paying the game number of times, I was able to ensure that the community was on the path of development and being well protected by the Risk Insurance in case of any adverse/unavoidable event
