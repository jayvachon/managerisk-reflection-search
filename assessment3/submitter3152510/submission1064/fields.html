The main goal of this game is to represent all the issues we have to deal with and all the factors involved when trying to apply it to risk management. Risk management is a concept hard to attain because it means we have to pay a balanced attention to all the factors that we need to protect and watch out. The first step we needed to consider was to contract an insurance service, as we knew all the expenses that being unprotected carried with it. It affected mainly, the money available for completing additional processes, such as developing a larger city, but indeed, provided a large benefit as it protected the city for a longer time, reducing the costs associated with reconstruction.

Being prepared means being aware of our surroundings and the impact they have on our daily life. As lived during the game, research should not be the only process we do to protect us, as if we do not have insurance service to recover from impacts, the money available will be less. In the same way, if we do not consider the amount of risk we are getting into, we are unable to measure our ability to protect ourselves and to have time to do so and act accordingly. Mainly, I think we should learn about the way these factors are interrelated so we can proceed and start making these adjustments in a proper manner and time. The links that tie the community are very important to be considered as part of the plan. The routine of creating a community where all the members are interrelated and work together to protect themselves in case of an event or crisis, helps to achive progress and development faster than the usual rate. Research has a direct link to these community ties, which means that as long as we have enough information from valid sources, we can take care of any communities as long as they need. Than means also that, in the real life, as long as we allow members of the community to take empowerment on their activities, they will be able to progress immediately.
