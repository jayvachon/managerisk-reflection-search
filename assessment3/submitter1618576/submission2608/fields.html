Generally, the better I understood the game and the risks the better I could protect against them and develop the community further. What were the main things you learned from the game? The better you understand the risks the better you can prepare against them, risk prevention is crucial to build a resilient community development. Also, research becomes crucial as allow the defender to tailor both insurance and protection to the particular risk affecting the community.
