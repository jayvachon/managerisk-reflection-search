Task 1

The role of risk management in the game was substantial and crucial. Without managing risks there will be no progress due to many negative events which continuously appeared. I have chosen a balanced approach by using knowledge, protection and insurance. I need to build as much as assets for assuring rise in cash flows needed for building new pods. I found that is much better to increase protection because insurance in situation when the same damages repeat only with difference of their extent. But also insurance is required due to the fact that the more pods you have build, the higher assets value you have to protect and without insurance this would be not possible. By small damages is near to irrelevant whether you have 30 or 75 percent insurance, but by big damages you will feel this difference.

Preparation for effective risk management affected development immediately, because both protection and insurance required money which could be invested into development of pods. As a reward for higher protection there was smaller damages accompanied by smaller financial requirements for the reconstruction of damaged pods.

Task 2

I think everyone playing this game first time is just expecting about how the game will develop and is trying to build more pods and protection whereas is no space for insurance. But it is a mistake of firstplayers.

If you are playing more times, you will found that you need to decide yourself more strategically about where invest accumulated money. I prefer strategy to invest for development of pods because they enable the increase of positive cash flow but immediately to build more protection. I invest less to insurance in times when I see less probability of damages due to knowledge acquired from observations of comets. So adding the observation results to decision making contribute to more balanced approach of risk management.

Main things I have learned by playing of this risk game are following:

a/ if you have really much assets, you will master any damages,

b/ if you have only few assets, even small damages can strongly diminish your assets value.

c/ you need knowledge and to understand the probabilities of negative events appearance,

d/ insurance is not only mean for speculation bringing rewards only for insurers, insurance is a right mean for protection of your assets. I think in this game was not allowed to buy 100 percent protection insurance, only 75 percent was the highest. But the more assets you own, the bigger the damages also in case that every negative event has only 20 percent impact on value of your assets.

Task 3

To say true in risk management are important only two things – you follow right risk management principles and you have right risk manager. I found in big banks and insurance even bad risk managers who were not able to identify risks and to prepare their institutions for adequate response, as well as managers who were in difficulties by deciding how much insurance to buy to cover expected risks adequately. So if you expect only small change in this game, I can propose one easy thing: the player as risk manager can choose whether to insure the damages of pods or to insure the damages caused by lack of knowledge due to lack of observations. The more observations you have, the less insurance you need. But the insurance for damages of pods will remain.
