This game shows risk management and its components: obtaining knowledge, protection, insurance. The success in this game is achievable only by using effective risk management strategies in combination with knowledge of the community development strategy. I spent resources on buying an insurance which covers 75% of losses and on community building. When I saw that a comet was coming I spent resources on studying it and  if its chance of  hitting and the severity of the comet was high  I tried to spent the rest of resources to boost community protection.

If I played the game only once, I would change the protection level and my community development strategy because I didn’t know from the beginning that to get to next level I should continue to develop pods which are already developed. First I thought that I should build all community and then to get to next level of development.  When I played the game more than once, I had experience and knowledge about the game. So it was easier to develop my community to the best of my ability while meeting certain milestones along the way. The preparation for risks helped me in development pods. The main things I learned from the game that effective risk management is not only knowledge of risks and protection from them but it is very important to have insurance because it can save money and resources in future. Of cause it is difficult to understand what kind of insurance to get because it seems to be a wasting of resources but in future insurance can save a lot and help in rebuilding the community quickly. That’s why I bought the insurance which covered the biggest amount of losses from shocks.

If I was tasked with developing a new version of the game, which allows me to change or add one simple thing I would changed the risks because in our life there is no only one type of risks as in this game. I would add more risks from the comet fall not only big waves but for example earthquake. In such way it would be more understandable the importance of protection and insurance in the planet life and it would be easier to teach players to spend resources on it and it also would add some dynamics to the game. More real risks and their consequences are better represent the risk management choices that matter in the our planet.
