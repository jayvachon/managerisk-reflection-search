The role of Risk Management into the game, was to allow the construction and the development of a community.

 

This role develops at three different interconnected stages:

1.       Analysis: Comets’ analysis, to collect more data as possible to face all risks related to them, and analysis related to the best insurance needed at different stages and in different situations;

2.       Construction Risks’ Management: in order to understand when is better to build new pods and when is better to develop the existing ones to better connect the community;

3.       Protection Management: to evaluate and organize long-term defences, analysing when they are needed and at what level, depending also on the development of construction.

 

Risk Manager must use two main tools to do his job into the game:

1.       Development bar, to analyse Milestones and understand if he is meeting his commitments;

2.       Interface devices, to understand and manage all instruments needed to risk management.

 

I managed the balance between risk management and construction dividing my work in three different phases:

1.       Construction and development: during calm phases, enlarging the community by building new pods or developing the pre-existing ones, depending on the situation;

2.       Long-term defence construction: during both phases, increasing it before bigger attacks, according to the development of the city/community.

3.       Comets’ analysis: during attacks phases, stopping development works to collect and  analyse available data to face further comets.

 

I have to admit that I often had to decide when I had to stop development to analyse comets’ data or build new defences, trying to find a balance to let people of the community work.

In some cases, I had to develop existing pods instead of building new ones, to better connect them, since it is also useful after attacks.

I have to admit that preparation for risks reduced construction’s speed.

 

I played the game four times.

At the beginning I was more concentrated on building pods and self defence, but then I understood how to better manage money increasing them and how to use them to balance all needs related to the game.

I decided, then, to start from a small community, obtaining a 50% insurance, not too expensive, but enough to speed contingent re-construction works, and to stop those works while analysing comets.

During my last attempt I was learning how to balance my work and my time facing urgent needs without forgetting my milestones.

 

I live in China, a densely populated Country, so I think that having a tool dedicated to alert population in those cases in which risk is higher should be important. I would like that this tool could also allow population to develop a self-protection network while moving forward into the levels, so risk manager will not only manage this tool, but teach local community how to manage by itself.

It means that at the beginning of the game Risk Manager should appropriately use this tool, during the game, then, the goal should be teaching the population how to use it by themselves.
