Risk management is the process of confronting risks, preparing for them and coping with their effects. In the game, and in the real life, we use risk management to achieve resilience in order to build prosperity.

During the game I have chosen invest in knowledge, because, as we have seen in this course, knowing what you are going to face has showed to be the best way to avoid risk. Using the knowledge obtained in the game I have builded the necessary protection for each situation, avoiding spending unnecessary cash and saving for the development of the village. I rarely had paid for insurance, only in situations that I really saw that the damage was going to be great. The process of development of my village occurred in a moderated time, always avoiding spending too much saving for shortage times.
