The role of Risk management in the game:

The role of risk management in this game is nothing but the trade-off between the “cost of opportunity (opportunity cost)” and protection of resources and is always followed by timely action.

The choices made:

In the game, as the opportunity cost (the money spent on Knowledge) of “Knowledge” was less and hence I did not consider it as a part of the trade-offs. But the trade-offs were between Insurance and protection during the beginning of each level, and I chose Insurance (the transfer of risk) to protection which worked will. During the course of the game and during the end of each level, the trade-off was between developing / upgrading the pods vs protection. For this particular trade-off I used the information obtained from the “Knowledge” by knowing the impact and probability.

Preparation for risk had direct impact on development. It stalled the development.

Learnings from the game:

I played the game twice. The first time I gave too much of importance to protection, which in turn stalled growth. The next time I was able to balance between all these trade-off’s and also was able to give more importance to community connections.

Things that can be improved in the game:

The game focused just to one risk and the strategic management for that risk with help of tools and trade-offs. But in reality this doesn’t happen. There will be many risks, some foreseen and the rest not, each occurring at various time periods or the same time period. So the game could include more risk factors and tools accordingly for better stimulation of the risk management.
