1.       The role of risk management in the game was twofold. One- To eliminate or minimize the risk. And Two- When you do in fact encounter it, how fast can you recoup? I have predominantly made the choice of developing existing pods rather than going for the new ones because this would eventually replenish my assets faster and I would have more dispensable cash in hand. The major trade off I have done is between Protection and Insurance. When I had sufficient protection I held off insurance until last seconds of comet being hit or I took the minimum possible insurance. Another trade-off is that instead of developing all pods at same rate I made sure some pods are perfectly developed before new ones are created. Preparation for risk affected development in this way-Protection helped in avoiding the extent of damage done by the comets and insurance helped in faster recouping when the comets hit the island.


2.       I played the game multiple times. What I did different from the very first time is that I concentrated on development of existing pods rather than creating new pods as this helps in faster replenishing of my assets faster. Secondly, I did not take maximum possible insurance as I have done while playing the game for the very first time and concentrated on protection.What I have learned from the game is that preparing for risk either helps us in eliminating the risk or at least we can pull ourselves together faster and cheaper in comparison to a state wherein we are unprepared or under-prepared.


3.       In the place I live, the major risk is uncertainty in the time and magnitude of rainfall. So I would consider an element of “Farming” on the island and amount of clouds in the sky with a risk of heavy wind blowing away the clouds eventually resulting in lower rain fall affecting the farm produce on the land. Moreover, another dimension of surprise-risk like Earthquake or Tsunami or Volcanic-Eruption can also be added. Risks like floods, drought, famine, asteroidal/comet collisions, etc. give us some idea of time of impact and degree of impact whereas others leave us without any beforehand information regarding the risk. So I personally think that adding that to the game would make it more interesting and realistic.
