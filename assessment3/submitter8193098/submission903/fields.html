In this game the main role of risk management was that there were different comets which could hit our pools which can cause damage to our pool. We had to save our pool from those comets in order to increase our level by building and upgrading pods. I had to create a balance between growing our community and preparing for these potential shocks.

The Power of Research

In terms of power of research I learned that the power to look out into space and learnt the chance of the comet hitting, the time until impact, and the severity of the comet. By clicking on a comet and keeping its box focused on screen, I begin to accumulate research. With each filled in notch, I receive more time to create community connections right before the comet hits, helping boost my protection.

Protection:

Our long term protection against the waves. I pressed and hold the slider, located on the bottom right of the screen, into the upright position to raise your island. By matching my level of protection, indicated by the meter to the right of the slider, with the severity of the comet, I prevented my pods from being damaged!

Insurance:

By obtaining insurance, a pod damaged from flooding was quicker and cheaper to rebuild. Insurance also recovers lost development time after my pods are repaired! I Clicked the insurance button at the bottom left of the screen to open the insurance purchase menu and select a plan.

 

By using these three powers in concert with development, it helped me to create a healthy community and create a Luminator legacy!

I played the game three times. First time I didn’t took any insurance and only used protection. And because of that reason I failed to reach further level and I lost at first level.

Second time I took insurance for 175 dollars and played but I didn’t used protection in the right manner but I reached level 2.

Third time I took insurance and used protection and did the research in right manner and I was able to reach level 3. After that I didn’t played the game.

If I would be asked to make the new version of this game I would not add anything that could more define risk management. The place where I live has same situation of risk management but there are no comets instead there are different situations.
