Risk And Opportunity: Managing Risk for Development.

The game aims to teach us how to confront a crisis, showing us, that with a short amount of money, we have to research for information, and then, try to look for protection, or some kind of insurance. The information about the problem is important, because it tells us, the severity of the problem, in order to think, if the better solution is in the look for protection, or  in the look for insurance. Look for protection looks like the best option, when it is an idiosyncratic risk, or in the game, when we were at the very first levels, while looking for some insurance, is better, when we face a systemic risk, like in the higher levels of the game.

As all the actions you take, cost you some money, you have to find the best combination between research of information, protection and insurance. In the game, as in the real life, exists a lot of trade-offs in the risk management, for instance, is not cost-effective to have a lot of protection, as well as the highest insurance. Another trade-off to highlight, is to invest your money to grow the city, or in the three elements listed above.

It seems that investing in risk management is very expensive, and while this is true, it ends up being cheaper. When a crisis hits you or you country, that investment begins to get noticed, reducing the damages that it leaves.

When I began playing, I just tried to enhance the city and buy some protection, as well as insurance, but then I realized, that first of all, I need to know the problem I’m going to confront, in order to take the corresponding action, also, I realized that in the very first levels, I have to protect my city of the hit of the meteorites, but in the higher levels, when I had a bigger country, if something bad does happen, the damages would be very expensive, so I invested in insurance too.

As I live in Colombia, in the case I have to add something to the game, I would decide to add some unexpected constraints throughout the game, trying to represent the hard environment and uncertainty that affect my country.
