The risk management role in this game was elaborated in many parts of the game’s techniques. First, it was in the offered insurance plans to reduce the cost of fixing the damaged pods in case of a negative shock. Also, the option to collect information about the comets along with the connection in the community that are made after each hit represented another image of risk management.

Knowledge was represented in the information collected for research on each comet. While protection was offered in different options such as the insurance that represented the protection of spending a large amount of money in case a disaster hit, the level of height of the area which also lower the degree of damage, and the research were made to reduce the probability of being hit by a comet and its level of severity. Finally, the different insurance plans represent the insurance element that is one of the effective risk management. Moreover, preparing for risk in advance helped in dealing more effectively with risks and negative shocks, which are main obstacles for development. It helped the development both immediately and after some time. Immediately was shown in how the community connections after the shock have worked together to prevent negative consequences. On the other hand, after some time was represented in collecting information for research about comets that helped after time to better understand the pattern of comets and how to reduce their probability of occurring, and the degree of how severe is the effect.

I have played the game more than once; therefore, what I did differently was focusing in developing the society while paying attention to the different risks facing the community. The main things I learned from the game are priorities what risk to deal with based on what has the highest probability of happening first. I also learned the importance of post shock procedures such as insurance, and community connections.

If I were to develop a new version of the game I would choose to add a feature of paying attention to the location of the pods. In Saudi Arabia, my home country, the locations of houses play a strong role in the possibility of facing destruction in case of floods. In addition, I would also add the factor of “corruption” as a risk, which is also another important factor effecting development in my country and many developing countries as well.
