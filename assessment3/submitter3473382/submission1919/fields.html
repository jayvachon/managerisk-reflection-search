1.What was the role of risk management in the game? What choices did you make and what trade-offs did you confront in terms of knowledge, protection, and insurance? In what ways did preparation for risk affect development (immediately, or after some time)?

Risk management was at the heart of the game ... from building communities (pods) to protecting the pods from comet impact and undertaking repair works to researching on the severity & chances of future risk related impacts to the communities.

I mostly had to build pods, protect them based on the feedback warning system embedded in the game that involved research, measurement of impact possibility & severity of impact should the negative event occur.

Tradeoffs: The loss of money was often traded off for the protection of the pods in order to ensure continued survival of the pods and winning the game within the time allotted.

The preparation for risks affected my development of the pod community immediately, because risks like the comet keeps coming at varying severity & possible impact, which often saw me focusing on the knowledge and protection as against building more pods.
