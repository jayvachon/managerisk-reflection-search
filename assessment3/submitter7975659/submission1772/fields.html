Reflections of the Risk Horizon Game

The role of risk management in the game was to develop a balanced community that was both protected from things such as natural disasters but also at the same time to build the community and take advantage of new opportunities. I had to learn how to balance all the factors involved. Knowledge was very important, with that I was able to research the comets and understand what their severity was, what the likelihood of the comet actually hitting was and research allowed me to protect my community. It was very important to have protection and insurance, without protection the chances of the comet hitting were much higher and the damage significant. By increasing my level of protection I was able to prevent a lot of comets from hitting and in the cases where there was a hit insurance was extremely important in order to repair the community in a timely and cost effective manner. A fine balance of all elements had to be achieved in order to properly develop and maintain the community.

I played the game multiple times and as I was playing I got better at it because I learned the importance of balancing, building the community, using upgrades and protecting and insuring the community. The first few times I tried different scenarios such as not purchasing insurance but using a high level of protection and doing more research but this was not successful since when the damage did incur the cost to repair the community was high and the time it took was very long hence I was unable to meet my milestones within the time period allotted. Then when I tried purchasing insurance and doing research but not using protection to raise my community I learned that was also not successful because the severity of the comets was very significant so there was always damage when a comet hit which resulted in a lot of repairs. At the end I had to balance all three elements, I made sure I bought insurance and used protection and always tried to raise the level of my community to meet the severity in order to avoid or at least lower the impact of the comet.Â  Research allowed me to build community links that also helped protect the community.

If I could change or add one thing to the game I would add another kind of risk. Right now the only risk was the comet but in reality we face other risks besides natural disasters so I would add another risk. An example would be to add workers and the possibility of workers losing their jobs and as you play the game you would have to access the likely hood of that occurring and then protect the worker against the risk by having other alternatives to earn a living in place. Overall, I really enjoyed the game it was a good way of learning risk management and how to balance different factors to ensure that you are protected against things that are not within your control.
