Risk Horizon game is based on how a corruption affects one's life. I have played game many times and used various strategies. I have tried to use all three options and came to the conclusion that, altough there must be a balance,  “protection” is the most important aspect of a risk management.

First, “insurance” was the priority in my risk management plan. I have bought %75 insurance plan and neglected information. Alora lived many corrptions because of not having enough information. Insurance  helped me to build up all pods of Alora with smaller amounts. When game was over, I had enough  “wealth” in order to build everything in Alora but had not enough time. That points a very important point: “Recovery time after a corruption”  must be taken into account for a risk management plan. is a very important  issue for a risk management plan.

Once, I tried to build only one “pod” and upgrade only this pod till the end of upgrade process.  It was another approach for me. I can explain this approach briefly as written below: “Amount of pods I have built must be equal to the amount pods I can protect”. This was also an unsuccesful strategy, because I could not develop Alora properly in a given time. I have used insurance and information. I have identified the importance of information in order to lower the effects of risk with especially minigame after the corruption. I have collected many information bars and had nearly  twenty second for many game but I did nothing in minigame. This made me to come to another conclusion: Informations about risks are placed in every where and every time but noboady want to develeop a project  according to the information that can be found in every report related with risk. So this is an another important point for me.

Last time, I have tried to use all three of the options. I have decided to make “protection” key factor of my  risk management plan. “Protection” levels war equal to the severity levels.  None of the pods are corrupted by meteor’s collapsion. “Minimum amaount of damage” is the.  main aim of a risk plan. Although it seems the most powerfull strategy, “protection” means more costs.  I also used information in order to upgrade protection levels. In real life experience, we have to compare the cost of protection and  recovery both in time and wealth.

I could not compare the cost of protection and recovery process in this game.  In order to better represent the risk management total cost of all pods must be shown in a separate box.

“Deleting some of the items” also is not allowed in the game. After all corruptions, I had to recover all pods. In real life expreinces, there may be some pods that we don’t want to repair after a corruption.
