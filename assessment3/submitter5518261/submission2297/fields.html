The Risk Horizon game is a real time strategy, which teaches how and why risk management is important for healthy development. In the game we need to think strategically and find the right balance between risk preparation and healthy development of the Alora. The balance can be only succeed by using three powers: of research, protection and insurance. We cannot neglect any of them.

Playing the game, first I tried to provide enough level of protection. I marked that it is easy to achieve the level of development for the first two levels and therefore I used the free time to improve the level of protection to be better prepared for the risk in the higher levels.

The second remark is related to the insurance. Once our community is not very developed (the first three levels) we do not need to invest money of this power. A high level of protection provides enough preparation for task affect development. In this way we can save resources and invest them for development. I was positively surprised that the high level of protection can safeguard the community and as a result it was no damages even if the comets were severe.

Conversely, the power of research gives us the necessary knowledge about the chance of the comet hitting, the time until impact, and the severity of the comet. This power makes complete the level of protection. Thus, I could select the danger, its intensity and as a consequence is better prepared for the risk. In the game, I concentrated mostly on the chance of the comet hitting- if it was highly predict I spent more time to learn about the potential danger. Then, the waves caused by the comets could not disturb the development of my community.

While playing the game the first time, I spent too much time on the substitutable development of the community without regarding of the protection. I wrongly found that the highest insurance level could prevent risk and I spent too much on building and upgrading pods. As a result I covered the whole area with pots but I did not enough resources to continue the development. The insurance can covered most of the damages but I suffered of lack of time by rebuilding. Therefore, I learned that we couldn’t perfectly plan and design the development of any community. The healthy development should be harmony and staggered and for it we need take into count potential risk as well.  

In the new version of the game I would have two kinds of improvement. First, I think useful to provide an exemplary video of the game. For the first time, I was working on the “slow” computer and I could not use all the functions in the correct way, specially the power of protection. I could achieve only the first level and I did not realize that the protection was the key of the game.

Secondly, I would like to increase of the importance of insurance. Actually, in the current version, we can only concentrate of research, development and protection without taking into consideration the insurance. I would create an additional risk, like an earthquake, which occurs rarely but unexpectedly and it would diminish the whole level of protection. However, we could not foresee it and only with the insurance we could effectively reconstruct the community.  On the other hand, the intensity and severity of comet hitting should be decreased in this case.
