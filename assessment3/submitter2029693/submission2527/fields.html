                                               Learning about Risk Assessment and Management - Risk Horizon

Question #1:
This game provided a good opportunity to playfully learn about the pros and cons of risk management. (provided risk management is complete) I would look at this game in two ways:
a. The risk was that of the impact of asteroids on this city and the implicated damage to the structures (structural and cash wise)
b. The developmental aspect was, generating cash as well as upgrading the structures such as to increase to cash generated, and then using the case to elevate the place of the structures such that it can shield the structures against the impact, as well as a possibility of insurance against the damage cause.

What I learned from my experience?
There is a definite trade-off between development and risk management, especially when it comes to sustaining an impact which might damage the structures and cost money.
Also, there is I found a balance point, if you develop and elevate the level of the structures as well as insure for as much damage as you can you can counter the impact, and even avoid any damage, but this will cost money and since money is the real measure of development, as is upgrading of the structures this trade-off is very stiff.
