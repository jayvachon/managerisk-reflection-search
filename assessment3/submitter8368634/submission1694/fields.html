Risk management is an important part of planning. The process of risk management is designed to eliminate the risk of certain kinds of events happening or having an impact. Risk management is a process for identifying, assessing, and prioritizing risks of different kinds. Once the risks are identified, the person in charge will create a plan to minimize or eliminate the impact of negative events. In the game, ALORA was victim to falling comets which affected the development. Strategies which I applied in order to increase the growth were:

 I tried to maintain a balance between growth and protection. I used insurance only when I had used the resources and had got full knowledge about the comet. If the comet’s strike rate was very high I used insurance and protected ALORA from high repair cost. I upgraded the pots in a synchronized manner and did not focus on the up gradation of just one pot, instead I upgrade them in a balanced way. Knowledge played an important role as it helped me to analyze about the strike rate, intensity of the comet and accordingly work upon the protection plan. ALORA’s main concern was that if the comets hit the water level would rise and damage the place. Hence the top most priority was protection. Raise the ground level and protect it from drowning. The strike intensity would get nullified and also insurance cost could be minimized or even eliminated. I implemented this technique and got better results.  

I did play the game more than once and learnt from my previous mistakes. The things that I did differently from first attempt were as follows:

·         I tried to make a balance between protection and development of pots. As I realized that development and protection go hand in hand. If I do not protect my pots from the comets, and keep on developing pots it will affect the whole area and destroy it. I will have a greater loss. Whereas if I make less number of pots and protect them they will provide me better return.

·         I should use my insurance only when required. Gather knowledge about the comets and then invest money on insurance. Wasting money without having knowledge is a wrong decision as it can be used somewhere else and get a better return.

·         Upgrade the pots wisely and steadily. Do not upgrade all together but upgrade them slowly but in pace with others. For example If I have upgrade one pot twice I should not upgrade it again instead upgrade the rest of the pots till 2nd level and then upgrade accordingly. This will give us profit uniformly and hence growth of the city.

Lesson learnt from this exercise:

·         Risk Management is necessary but more important is coping from the after effects once harsh conditions hits.

·         How wisely one can think and use the resources in an effective way such that it acts as an advantage.

·         Prevention is better than cure.
