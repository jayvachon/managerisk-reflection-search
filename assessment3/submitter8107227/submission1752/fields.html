*The risk horizon game has many roles, one of the roles are to use all the resources you have to decrease the loss possibilities as much as you can while you facing the risk, some of those resources is: 
1- Knowledge: by understanding the shock and how it will effect the thing and having your own view about the side effects ( positive and negative effects) also understand the internal and the external conditions and the potential outcome.

2- Insurance: insurance is very important  it is one of the main parts of the risk management because it is transfer the resources across the people by sharing the risk so that the loss will be divided by many people which minimize the effect of the loss because some times the cost of the the loss is much bigger than than the people afford and it might effect country or the person badly for long time, so in this case the cost of the insurance is because necessary to take even it is very high and in the other hand we can measure the risk by the insurance if the risk is high the cost of the insurance is also high in most of the cases.

3- Protection: it is about reducing to probability and the size of the cases and increase the benefits.

4- Coping: it is about making the most of the benefits of the loss, for example there are many countries faced a big crisis but because the well management and forecasting of the future they change the loss to a great opportunity and they pass the crisis and they became one of the most important economy in the world.
