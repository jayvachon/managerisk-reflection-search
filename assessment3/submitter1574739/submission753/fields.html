The game is balance between risk preparations and developing your community.

The whole idea is to reach a level of growth by develop the buildings and at the same time you have to keep your colony safe from a known danger.

It is crucial to have available a minimum amount every time, in order to keep community safe. The necessary amount can only be produced by developing your community level.

Like in the real word, it is difficult to keep resources outside the production process for reasons, such as emergency situations and increase and decrease these levels depending on the level of risk and imminent disaster result.  

 If you spend too much on building and upgrading pods, you will be exposed to comets when they hit. When you sustain damage from comets your progress bar on the left is decreased. On the other hand, if you spend too much on preparation for risk, you will not have enough money to develop your community sufficiently to pass the level.

The game is a very simplified version of the actual conditions but the general principles are the same. The knowledge of imminent dangers together with insurance resources and protection actions (at the stage of preparations) are able to provide recover from losses and make the most of benefits (coping stage).
