I learned in this game that how to balance between three Luminator powers: Research, Protection, and Insurance. By using three powers i.e. Research, Protection and Insurance, I learned how to create a healthy community and a Luminator legacy. I also learned in the game that how to prepare against negative shocks in this case comets, by means of allocating knowledge characteristics of each comet, protection which is ground level and insurance meaning resources to rebuild broken pods, all this done in a balanced way and given an initial amount of financial resources, while at the same time being able to foster development, that is to say, making the pods evolve over time. If a comet did hit the ground, another goal was to learn how to cope with its effects by recovering from the loss using the same elements. Some of the trade-offs I had to face were, for instance, giving up a higher insurance coverage in exchange for more protection and more resources to build new pods, or having to build less pods in order to be able to afford of their insurances when they evolved since they became more expensive. So as a consequence of these trade-offs, this led me to make some choices which had to do with reaching a balance between the deployment of knowledge, insurance and protection, thus making sure these elements were available every time and that they did not turn into obstacles. However this was an effective decision since costs of preparation ended up being lower than costs of coping. After playing the game several times, I learned that the finest way to manage risk while encouraging development is by identifying the resources you have and how that may help you mitigate risk, making an even and moderated use of them, implementing development actions gradually but strongly, thinking strategically, and learning from experience to become more resilient every time towards the effects of risk In contrast to this, when I had done wrong the first time I played this game was to focus right straight into the development without taking enough time to analyze hot to manage preparation so that development would not be hindered. Development was effected by preparation for risk in times when the sever comets with high chance of hit came closer to the ground, thus forcing me to spend great amounts of financial resources on preparation which did not allow me to achieve a continuous development.
