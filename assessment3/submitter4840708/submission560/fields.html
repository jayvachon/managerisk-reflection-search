1) In this game we had to manage risks concerning with threat of falling comets. In particular, we had to evolve our city and simultaneously protect it from risks (falling comets). I was making such decisions: what to build, to buy insurance or not, to spend money to ascend land or to upgrade buildings, to spend time on research or to enhance protection. Preparation for risk affected  development by having to make the protection better and that slowed the evolution of the city. Also, insurance policies need money what I could spend to upgrade or buy buildings.

2) When I failed the first time, the next one I tried to look better at maintaining the balance between buying insurance policies, building pods, develop protection and making research of comets. Also I tried to devote more attention to risk protection and insurance policies, especially the risk protection. This happened because I saw, that if I have good protection against comets, in bulk of cases I have no damage and this is pretty good :)   I spent money to insurance policies only if I noticed the comet with very great damage and very high possibility of falling in the sea near my island. Also I  took into account the speed of comets. If they flew fast I research them rather then spent money to enhance protection.

 

3) I want to add to game different shocks. Not only falling comets, but, for example, fires, hurricanes, storms, earthquakes, plagues etc. In my opinion, this things make the game more realistic. Also it would be good if this different disasters will be connected with each other to make sinergetic effect. This cause more different methods of protection that can be added to the game if we will use different shocks.
