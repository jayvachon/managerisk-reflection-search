The implementation of strong and effective risk management and controls within securities firms promotes stability throughout the entire game. The implementation protects the city in related losses (e.g., firm failure, misappropriation, fraud, etc.);

 

In execution of the risk management control, the development of the city was immediately performed; insurance, to discounts on possible losses, comes next, followed by protection, secure the city from being damage, and knowledge, to re-strategized and project what will happen.

 

I have played the game twice; the strategies that were implemented are as follows:

  1. Use the resources wisely – making sure that there is always an enough resources to supplement the development, repair, purchase of insurance, security and research.
  2. Prioritize the development – reaching the goal and completing the green portion of the meter.
  3. Get an insurance – to protect the city from possible losses; the insurance also recovers the lost development.
  4. Securing the city – making sure that it white bar in the left portion reached level 4;
  5. Knowledge – re-appropriation of resources if there will be time.

 

Below are the main things that I have learned from the game:

  1. Develop risk assessment and mapping.
  2. Outline the responsibilities
  3. Identify and define the risks
  4. Determine the threat level and focus on the risk that has the greatest potential to affect the city.
  5. Establish levels of controls for processes commensurate with the perceived threat.
  6. Record and retain risk incident and near-miss information.
  7. Conduct assessments to determine changes profile and assess performance.
