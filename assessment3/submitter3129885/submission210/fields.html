1.       What was the role of risk management in the game? What choices did you make and what trade-offs did you confront in terms of knowledge, protection, and insurance? In what ways did preparation for risk affect development (immediately, or after some time)?

Risk management, in forms of research, protection, and insurance played a vital role in ensuring that the gains from the development were sustained in face of adverse shocks, enabling progress to higher levels. Development undertook the form of building and upgrading pods, knowledge in form of research to understand the severity and probability of comet strike, protection in form of raising the level to reduce the flooding impact, and insurance to reduce the coping costs. The choices were to spend resources in form of money and time, on development, insurance, protection or research. Preparing for risk in form of research, insurance or protection diverted resources from immediate development but paid off in form of more time to prepare for the adverse events and/or reduced impact of the adverse impact as well as insurance coverage covered parts of the repairs leading to lesser burden on resources in post impact period aiding faster recovery.

 

2.       If you played the game only once, what would you do differently if you were to play it again? Alternatively, if you played the game more than once, what did you do differently after the first time? What were the main things you learned from the game?

The main things I learnt from the game was that there should be a balance between various aspects of risk management while making sure that immediate development needs are also being met and resources are not over-concentrated on risk management. Also preparation is necessary to get one selves and the community ready to handle shocks without adversely impacting development or ensuring that the impacts are minimal and recovery is quick. When I first played the game, I spent too much resources (in hindsight) on protection, but realized from my failures and later game plays that a balance is needed in all the aspect of risk management and being prepared and insured is as important as being prepared, and most importantly developing is important to make sure you meet the requirements to progress to the higher levels in the context of the game or better standards of life in real life context.

 

3.       The game captures, in a simplified way, some aspects of risk management. Imagine you are tasked with developing a new version of the game, which allows you to change or add one simple thing. What change would you propose to better represent the risk management choices that matter in the place you live?

Though the game covers all the aspects of risk management beautifully, one aspect that could be added is the risk-opportunity dualism. What I mean is that some of the shocks could turn out be opportunity rather than risk, and one needs to be well prepared to cope in both the eventualities, to make sure that losses from risks are minimized, and development does not suffer large setbacks as well as when opportunity comes, one is prepared to make full use of opportunity in terms of having adequate knowledge and resources to gain from the opportunity and make the progress to a better standard of life or get rid of some risks altogether. Possibly low levels of floods could be beneficial for production of salt or leading to increase in fish catch or some such scenario which could be developed so that some of the comet landings could be beneficial also.
