Risk management plays a main role in the game. Thanks to it, the village is able to prevent or reduce the negative effects of risk and to develop with a very fast pace. In the game, there are three fundamental parts of Risk management: Knowledge ( research ), protection (raising the ground) and insurance. The game shows how importnat is to find the right balance between these three elements because a perfect knowledge needs time and it is also necessery to build protection, so is importnt to make a decision about how to spend time. Another trade off is about money because both insurance and protection are expensive and resource are limited. moreover they also have an impact on the development of the village: money are actually necessary also for development. as we learned in a past video, this is a obstacle for risk management because in the short term there are only costs that are visible (less resources for development) and it is posible to see benefits only after some time.
