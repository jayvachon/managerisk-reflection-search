The role of risk management in the game is all about thinking strategically in all areas. You have to balance the preparation between different risks, and investing in development of the community and bearing in mind the tools you have accessible to you. It is like multitasking among the different projects so as not to allow the other project suffer at the expense of the one being focused on. It is very important to develop a community and then, strategize. There should be adequate research and development, it helps and when the shocks come, there should be adequate insurance and upgrading as well as research.  To succeed, you have to assess the obstacles of risk management, once you have gathered some of these information, you are able to reduce most of the uncertainty about the risk. Risk  is composed by two elements, (probability) and impact (consequences)and the coping of the after effects. The impact could be maximized or minimized by the external environment, internal conditions and of course, the responsible and effective grade of risk management. So, when you reduce the uncertainty of risk, identifying its causes, (drivers of shocks) and estimating a possible severity. After that you could treat the different causes of risk with high severity and then begin to outline the possible policies, programs or activities to exonerate the risk. Sometimes you may design actions to lessen only the probability, other time you could only lessen the impact or even both of them. There is also the control design which is targeted at the response to each step in what is known as the "risk chain", There could be control measures to alert us when risk occurs, we can have preventive measures to prepare us towards the phase. All these could be considered as a strategic planning model which could be applied to life and business. It is paramount to make the necessary research, understand the risk and shock involved and design the possible ideal actions to cope with the after effects. The goal is to eradicate or lessen the probability of risk and treat the causes of risk.  We can also talk about the protective measures to lessen the impact and corrective measures ideal to cope with the risk and after effects.
It is also about having the right resources at the all time. Without the right or adequate resources to manage these projects and developments, it is quite difficult to move ahead. No project has more priority than the others, you just have to invest adequately to most if not almost al of the developments.

I learnt from playing the game that you need to make adequate research on whatever setbacks that you are prone to have. The risks that you are prone to have should be given adequate research on to understand how to handle, confront and cope with.

I would rather modify the frequency of the risks coming upon a community in the sequence at which it came. There might be communities who suffer risks in that design but I feel the frequency at which the risks were coming upon the community was too quick.
