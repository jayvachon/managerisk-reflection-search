Preparing for risks and taking opportunities to balance the world

The game sets the player directly from the beginning in to the role of risk management. But this alone will not help to advance into higher levels. To achieve greater success in the game as well as in real life one has to take the opportunities to develop society and surroundings as well. Without the balance of resources to both parts neither part will be successful. A more developed society will give better knowledge into protection of assets as wells as coping with the results of a disaster. This would be a part I would have changed in a future version of the game. As an example the more the player researches the comets the less time will be used to raise the land. To get a more balanced game it should be changed that the faster you raise the land the more expensive it will be.

Another important aspect is insurance. It helps to cope with the outcome of any disaster. To help the poorest regions in the world to afford such insurance different models of insurance are already available. The Takaful insurance system of the Islamic world is established for a long period and depends on co-operation. Losses and profits will be shared equally between members. This can reduce significantly. The game reflects this by providing community links the player can join after a comet has hit and flooded his world.

As I have played the game some multiple times I have learned that different approaches will result in very different outcomes. Focusing on a low amount but highly developed plots increases costs to rebuild after an evitable strike. But will also reduce means, time and/or money, to raise the world or research comets. On the other side a high amount of lowly developed plots will reduce the needed means to repair, but will also reduce growth in further levels. Time runs out before the game world will advance.

Only the right balance of knowledge, insurance and resources spent will help in terms of prevention, preparation and actual coping after a disastrous event. I will take this with as an outcome of the game and apply in future strategy decisions to be made.
