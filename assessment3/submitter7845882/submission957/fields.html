In this game, the role of risk management is to minimize the adverse outcome of risks to the greatest extent. I chose to buy insurance and gain the knowledge of how server the upcoming risks are, and the probability of occurrence the risks are in order to minimize the adverse outcome of risks. If we have good risk management and preparation, we will get minimal risk and quickly recover to previous development.

I played the game three times. The first time I played the game, I bought small percentage of insurance, but in the second and third times, I bought bigger percentage of insurance to minimize the cost of recovering. I’ve learned that there a significant numbers of ways to prepare and minimize risks, such as: gathering all relevant information about upcoming risks, buying insurance, cooperating with neighboring   communities, etc.
