Risk management in the game was important in the sense of evaluating the impact and takes some measures as a conclusion of the assessment of the risk even if that means to prepare for the impact or to ignore the impact and take the opportunity to continue to construct (develop). Depending on the situation and of potential damage that was estimate during the research of the comet the game learn you how to play with measures even in the real life, because in function of the situation you could take measures in terms of knowledge an protection, or protection and insurance or knowledge and insurance, and so on. Because preparation for risk has an immediate impact on the budget then the development is affected immediately then if for example we don’t take any preparation for a risk.

 

First time when I played the game I was focused in prepare for the risk, and I didn’t pay attention to development, and in level 3 when the treats are multiple I’ve failed to touch my objective which was the development. Then in second attempt I’ve tried to keep in balance the assessment of risk and the development with my main focus oriented on development. So, what I’ve learned from the game is that you have to keep a balance between assessment of the risk and development. If you are focused on risk too much you could loose yourself in preparation for risk and forget about your goal.

 

I think that in order of understand better the risk management it should be more obvious what are the opportunities, because risk doesn’t mean only treats (and thoes are very well represented here by the comets which you could translate in any kind of real treats from natural disaster to politics or economic ones). Should be more obvious related to opportunities how to evaluate them, how to catch them, how to accelerate the apparition of opportunities.
