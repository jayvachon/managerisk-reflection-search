                                        MY REFLECTIONS FROM THE RISK HORIZON GAME

Risk management played a vital role in the game in that to wine and go to the next level of the game; you had to bear in mind that you are dealing with a very serious situation that if not properly managed, could lead to the collapse of the whole system (you being won). For example, if you were not steady and knew what risk management was all about, you could focus just on protecting your pods while neglecting the importance of insurance and research. This will cost you greatly once you in-cure any damage. Having insurance and a little bit of protection goes a long way to help in risk management. For example, even if your pods were damaged, the damage was less if you were protected, thus easily repaired.

I had to make lots of choices in order to manage risks. Resources was always limited and with this knowledge,  I had to keep searching for incoming comets, keep raising my protection level and buying  insurance to cover damages caused on my communities (pods). Therefore, I had to trade-off the resources I had for knowledge, protection and insurance.

Preparation for risk management can positively affect development, e.g., make community connection before a disaster, and spend more time on research, especially for severe comets or issues that have plagued the community in the past. The more research, the more time one will have to prepare.  Research shows that with the current technology, we can lift an entire community up and out of flood’s paths. This can be done for example by; putting houses on stilts, lay down sandbags or build dams to protect homes from flood. Thus preparation for risk can affect development immediately or after some time as was seen in the game.

An important aspect of the game was the point that everything we do has some cost, either in time or resources, and such is the way of the world where nothing comes easy. Some forms of shocks include; floods, famine, drought, economic collapse   or a small household shock like losing a job. When a shock is imminent, it might be too late to protect or buy insurance. Therefore, community connection is very important. However, diminishing returns are a fact of life here. Protecting the community is useful, but can get expensive, for the higher the community rises, the more expensive it becomes to raise it further.

It was observed from the game that community protection is a type of insurance, in that when a disaster strikes, we help each other survive by sharing resources Thus, forming and sustaining connections is valuable.

I played the game more than once. What made me go through the next levels was the point that I applied all the risk management tools learned from the course. Initially, I was not seeing the importance or using research, protection and insurance all together. I finally discovered that I could not do without insurance and paid highly for damages I incurred without insurance.

If I were to revise the game, I would have added a section for community harmony, where people will not only work as individuals, but will work together, knowing that failure for example, by a single person to pay insurance for his property can go a long way to affect the entire community.
