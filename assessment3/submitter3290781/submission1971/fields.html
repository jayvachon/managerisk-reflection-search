What was the role of risk management in the game? What choices did you make and what trade-offs did you confront in terms of knowledge, protection, and insurance? In what ways did preparation for risk affect development (immediately, or after some time)?

 The game was about making a choice of the level or insurance, protection and information gathering. In the beginning I was trying to maximise the insurance and the protection and paid little attention to the information regarding the probability and the impact of the risk, and I could not complete level 3,as insurance just minimised the financial impact of destructions but did not reduce their occurrence. Then I realised that I should start developing protection and in parallel obtain the information, in order to decide on my actions. I used insurance in a couple of times when the impact was very high and the probability of occurrence more than 40%, and in parallel I tried to increase the level of protection with one additional point for risk minimisation. I did not take any action for risks with impact less then 3 or risks with medium impact and low probability.

If you played the game only once, what would you do differently if you were to play it again? Alternatively, if you played the game more than once, what did you do differently after the first time? What were the main things you learned from the game?

 As mentioned above I played the game numerous times until I managed to reach level 5. In the beginning I was focusing on obtaining insurance, the build protection and paid little attention to the information. Then I realised that I should start building basic protection and then analyse the information before taking any action. In general the primary goal was to increase protection in order to deal with the impact of the risk, and add insurance in the cases of very high impact. Therefore I learned that information is key and we need to find the right balance between protection and insurance.

The game captures, in a simplified way, some aspects of risk management. Imagine you are tasked with developing a new version of the game, which allows you to change or add one simple thing. What change would you propose to better represent the risk management choices that matter in the place you live?

 In general the game provides the opportunity to trade off between protection, insurance and information. What is not very clear is the financial resources increase. There is no rule or at least a clear one on how the money available is increased over time and sometimes it seems that they are infinite. Living in southern Europe the last 5 years made me realise that resources and more particular financial resources are not unlimited. Therefore, if I could change something in the game would be the way that resources are available and are increased over time.
