'PREVENTION IS BETTER THAN CURE", this proverb is indeed apt in every aspects of our lives. With modernization, globalization and other forms  of development, the world is more prone to disasters. It is not only the natural calamities which pose risk to the world, but also other man made things which devastate  their own kind. Hence such uncertain  events have to be met in a more systematic and organized way so as to reduce the severity and several lives being taken away. Here comes the role of RISK MANAGEMENT.RISK MANAGEMENT is indeed very important to tackle any calamities or uncertain events. The possibility of the event has to be projected well in time and communicated to the folk who confront the same. At the same time necessary measures should be taken so that the severity of the event would not affect the lives.This game enables one to prepare an uncertain event and also reduce the severity of the event. Indeed it is a well developed virtual game where one can get an idea as to how to confront a calamity in a more efficient way.In this game a certain region is exposed to a SHOCK,  with the available options one should try to confront the risk of shock in an efficient manner.to trade off the risks the following strategy may be adopted:the knowledge about the comet or shock has to be knownsince every pod development involves cost, it has to be reduced by taking an insurancethe insurance enables reducing costs of development and repair of each podsAt the first instant, one would always try developing as many pods as possible, but it will lead to only destruction, hence insurance has to be taken.the main things learned from the game areprotection is always advisable in any forms of events, hence insurance plays a crucial role to avoid more costsThe calamity need not be always taken from one angle which is loss or suffering, there is a opportunity attached, which has to be taken as an advantage and there on work toward the samelearning from past events is very essential which would enables to prepare for a future calamity in a more efficient manner For a beginner this game gives a lot of take away in a generic way. the game can be developed with a specific natural calamity or an event, with varied options.
