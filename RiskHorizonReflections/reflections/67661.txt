1) What
 was the role of risk management in the game? What choices did you make 
and what trade-offs did you confront in terms of knowledge, protection, 
and insurance? In what ways did preparation for risk affect development 
(immediately, or after some time)?The role of risk management in the game was to
identify and lessen problems encountered when trying to build a
community of houses. 

The choices I made were to use all of the three
forms of risk management sparingly but not focus on one in
particular. For example I would take some (but not the full)
insurance, protect by raising the island a few bars and spend a
little time researching each comet. I adapted the strategy slightly
for each level so in later levels I would buy more expensive
insurance if the likelihood and severity of a comet hit were both
high. 

For me, preparation for risk affected development
because I would often wait to see if a comet hit before deciding
whether to build a new house. There was no point starting to build a
