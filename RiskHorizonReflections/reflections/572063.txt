The role of risk management in the game was about building a community but, with the risk of protecting it from the meteors. To overcome this, a heavy research was conducted on the behavior of the meteors in order to ensure its severity level and the community was insured as well to cope with the damages.The first time my intention was only to expand the community whereas the next time it was about learning about the risk , protecting the community and also efficiently coped up with the consequence of a bad hit.
