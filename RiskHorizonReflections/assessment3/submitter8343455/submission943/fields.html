Game Code = MTFS35V

The main purpose of the game is to effectively and efficiently manage risk and its consequences.  It is the successful balancing of development of a community with protecting that community against threat such as natural disaster or the like or any kind of risk.

 The role of risk management in the game is the prediction and management of the probability and severity of risk (catastrophic events) on a community. 

·         The prediction of the risks is through researching the risks and predicting/estimating the likely of those risks happening and their severity and consequences on the community.  The impact/consequences can adversely impact on the development of the community and set it back on its development progress.

·         The management of the risks consists of developing a community by building it from grass root and protecting it against the risks (comets threat – wave are generated by comets hitting a large water body where a village of pod sit as an island. This is akin to the impact of earthquake), via efficient observation of the probability and severity of the risk, and managing that probability and severity through:

o   insuring (purchase of insurance) the community for reconstruction/rebuilding post the disastrous consequences of the risk hen it occurs;

o   Working with the community (community connection) through share information and resources soon after the event and even during the events;

o   Protecting the village via construction of infrastructure – in this case it is consisted of raising the village to reduce the impact of wave after a comet hit. In other case such as flooding, it will be construction of levees/stopbanks to keep flood water in the river or to enact legislation that requires building to be built in some particular areas and not others, and/or to have the building floor level at certain height.

Preparation for risk help prepare ensure that the pods are not affected after a comet hit or if affected the level of impact is minimal and recovery is fast and a low cost.  Preparation ensure all steps are taken to insure the community and build adequate infrastructure to protect the community.  Good preparation gives confidence to build the community and lessens the fear that paralyses against development.  The experience through the game is that with less preparation/research, the comet threads are regular and their impacts damaging and effort and resources are wasted on rebuilding instead of developing the community.

My first attempt on the game was spent on focusing on one component of protection – raising the village.  The second attempt was spent on trying to develop, but the comets hits and subsequence damages sapped all my resources.  I then learn that the basis is to concentrate on balancing development with protection against comets. Protection against comets requires good research of their probability of impact and severity of those impacts.  The more I research the confident I became and can develop while protecting and insuring.
