The game taught us to employ the tools of risk management viz. knowledge, protection, insurance and coping. Acquiring knowledge about the risk was time consuming but it was helpful in minimizing the damage once the risk materialized. Knowledge was necessary in order to know in advance about the severity of risk and its probability of materializing which was in turn useful was acquiring sufficient level of protection and insurance. At times when research was missed for a risk the damages to development were huge and coping mechanism was also adversely affected. There was a trade-off between spending time on acquiring knowledge and taking up development (building and upgrading pods) activities and time had to be very judicially divided between the two activities as too much of time spent on acquiring knowledge was hampering development.

In the first attempt, I completely missed the importance of research and community networking after the risk had materialized. Even though I was doing well on development front but since I was not adequately researching about the risk the damages were massive and were undoing all that was done. I was focusing majorly on protection and insurance which was not sufficient for managing the risk comprehensively. In the subsequent attempts, I started spending time on research as per the probability of risk materializing. If the probability was high I would spend a higher time on research and thus I would get more time for coping for the risk once the risk had materialized. Another thing that I was not doing right in the earlier attempts was that I was being too risk averse and I was not spending sufficient time on developing resources because I was too apprehensive of the risk when the probability of it materializing or its predicted severity was not very high. Key things I learnt were –

·  Risk management is necessary in order to pursue development opportunities

·  Preparation in terms of acquiring knowledge about the risk is the key in order to assess the severity and probability of the risk materializing

·  Protection needs to be adequate and should be commensurate with the anticipated severity of the risk

·  Insurance is necessary for providing for funds required for mitigating losses once the risk has materialized

·  Once the risk has materialized community networking is a critical step towards coping and a good preparation leads to a more efficient coping

All the risks presented in the game in form of comets were unavoidable and there was no choice but to manage them but in the present version there is no element of voluntary risk taking. If I were to add one thing in the game, that would be an option to take on some risks voluntarily. That could be in form of constructing some structures that would double the rate of development but if they, say, exploded then the damage would have been twice as much. Accordingly knowledge, protection, insurance and coping measures could have been built in.
