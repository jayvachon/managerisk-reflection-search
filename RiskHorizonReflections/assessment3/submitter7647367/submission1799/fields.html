The role of the risk manager in the game is to use resources to nullify the risk/danger by preparation or capacity to prepare, utilising resource to ensure safety of human and physical assets.

Knowledge was obtained from experience while playing the game. Research of the various risks or possible disasters helped to make decisions to spend resource to protect against and/or avoid risks.

Protection was used to raise the island to avoid or lesson the impact of risk. This costs money and use thereof drains resources and must be used wisely and sparingly, yet effectively.

Insurance can be bought with money and at the expense of other resources. Insurance must be bought wisely as one can over or under insure.

Preparation for risk requires resources and money, therefore slowing development down. Under preparation ensured disaster and destruction and loss of infrastructure.
