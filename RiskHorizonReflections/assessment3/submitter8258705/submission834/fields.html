1) The Risk Horizon game emphasizes the importance of risk management to get healthy development. Because of the falling comets, it is mandatory to adopt risk management tools (knowledge, protection, and insurance) to guarantee the strong development of the community. However, preparation for risk can undermine development because risk management costs resources and time that could be used to develop the community. An important lesson of the game is that community cannot survive without producing resources. At the beginning of the game, it is more important to develop the community by building and upgrading pods than invest in risk management tools. However it is mandatory to build progressively all the foundations for risk management that you need to manage the potential shocks that hinder development.

 2) I played this game twice: the first time, I was focused on managing the risk and I forgot about developing the community. The second time, I adopted a more balanced position between growing measures on the one hand and risk management instruments on the other hand. I already talked about the importance of resources for risk management highlighted in this game.The Risk Horizon game also emphasizes the existence of a block of risk management tools: knowledge, protection and insurance. It is impossible to accomplish two actions at the same time and trade-offs have to be made. Thanks to knowledge, it is possible to get information about the risks and adopt the best risk management tool against it. It is then possible to take an insurance or protection. In the game, the risk of falling comets is too high to use insurance as a permanent solution. Another lesson of the game was that: “when the risk is high and frequent, insurance is not a solution. Only protection can take care of very repeated and frequent risks”. But the game also showed that not all the shocks should be prevented.  If we dedicate all the resources get the best protection available against the risk, it is impossible to develop the community.  In that case it is more efficient to cope afterwards and tools as insurance can help facilitate a quick response following the shock. Another lesson of the game is that: “the more advanced and expensive is a technology, the higher is the price to insure it and protect it against risks.

 3) If I had to develop a new version of the game, I would include some risks impossible to anticipate. It would better represent the risk management choices that have to be done. Indeed, risk management is not only about expected and anticipated risks. It is impossible to anticipate everything. That is the reason why it is important to put in place processes to keep flexibility and ability to react to unexpected things and cope afterwards.
