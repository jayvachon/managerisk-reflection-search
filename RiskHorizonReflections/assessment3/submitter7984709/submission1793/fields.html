It seemed that protection had minor effects on controlling or prohibiting the shocks. On the contrary, knowledge had an incremental effect minimizing the impacts probability. I started first round by focusing on protection and the outcome was more impact. This brought me to the understanding that high level protection cannot guarantee less shock as it has no effect on reducing the chance of impact.

Then I diverted attention on insurance which seemed to bring more confidence especially after the attacks, but again there was no control over the impact and it was just like spending unlimited amount of budget just to alleviate the pain after the shock.

It was in the next rounds that I experienced the power of knowledge. The more I researched about the nature of the comets; I could better confront the impact of comet. The number of impacts and the consequences of shock reduced as a result of paying more attention to the knowledge around the natural events.

This also showed that preparation for risk was possible through a series of actions that should be taken through the process of risk management. Immediate reaction did not reduce the risk of impact, because there was no plan behind immediate reaction and it was just an unplanned response to the disasters I was facing.

If I was supposed to play the game again, research was the first action I would start with, and then I would put a lot of energy on communication, as I found communication a productive way of sharing the knowledge that was useful in minimizing the impact of disaster. Knowledge sharing and contribution are main instruments after we face shock every time.

What the place I live in suffers from is lack of a leadership in risk management when facing a shock. Risk management process is not very well orchestrated and resources are not allocated properly. I personally believe that risk must be managed like a project, meaning that, it must have an owner who is responsible and makes decisions and instructs all available resources in order to make the risk a success, minimizes loss and puts the team in place of opportunity. Next very important aspect of risk management is the lessons learned from every case. This can help better manage any similar future shocks, reduces the level of risk and reduces any future cost may incur from similar accidents. Thus I would not add anything to the game if I was tasked to develop a new version, instead I will put more attempt to leading and lessons I can learn from the game.
