The role of risk management in the game was to make the player take the responsibility in the game to handle the funds at their disposal, judiciously and reduce the adverse effects of damage(caused due to  comet crash and flood).

Through this game, I got the basic sense of how to take risks by keeping in mind the worst possible outcomes and preparing for them well in advance.As i played the game over and over again, i came to the realisation that investing in insurance was the best way to go about the game since it helped me enormously to reduce the damage caused by various factors and i didn't have to fork out alot of money in repairing the damages since most of it was already covered.Preparation for risks paid in huge amounts- by researching the path of the comets, I was able to decide how much to invest on the development of the pods and how much damage these pods would undergo depending upon the 'severity' and 'chance of hit' of the comet.Researching and insurance went hand in hand with development and helped me to create and develop my planet more than i could have ever done without these assets at my disposal.

After playing the game again and again , I learnt that investing in insurance was worth every penny as it prepared me to tackle the aftereffects of the damage with less adversity. As i replayed the game over and over, I also realised he techniques to tackle the comets and reduce the damage caused by them by researching each and every one of them keeping in mind the severity of damage they were to cause in case they would 'hit' my planet.As I entered the higher levels of the game,I  understood the fact that
