Risk Horizon Game reflection

Risk management helped in preventing or reducing damage to the development. In order to progress with the development without incurring any damages, tradeoffs had to be confronted for instance, delaying the development whilst protecting the development. Investing in the protection of the development is another choice that had to me made, in order to prevent or reduce the risk of exposure to the falling comets.

Preparation for the risk affected the development in such a way that more investment had to be made in the protection of the development which could have been used in increasing the development. If the development was affected by the fallen comet, the level of protection decreased, as such extra protection had to be incurred however, maximum protection reduced the damage caused by the comets such that one could repair the damage and continue with the development. Maximum insurance had to be purchased in order to protect the development, this reduced the amount invested in the development but eventually it helped in overcoming the shocks that were incurred due to the damaged caused by the comets. Research consumed a lot of time which could have been used to finish the developments fast but eventually the knowledge gained helped to know the amount of risk the development was exposed to which helped in maximizing the protection or not in order to prevent damage from the falling comets.

Playing the game more that once helped me avoid the mistakes I made in the first trial, such as forgetting to protect the development as well as to purchase insurance. In the game, I learnt that protection, insurance and research are important in preventing damage to the development.

I would propose a change that could also capture rivers flowing through the communities. This type of rivers results to frequent flooding in my country. It could be helpful if protection of developments from risks that could result from physical features that exist amongst communities was included in the game.
