What was the role of risk management in the game? What choices did you make and what trade-offs did you confront in terms of knowledge, protection, and insurance? In what ways did preparation for risk affect development (immediately, or after some time)?

Risk management was critical in mitigating the affects of the regular bombardment and destructive force of the settlement by meteorites. Risk management in the game was concerend with making sure that the settlement was able to continually grown and develop as best as possible in the face of risk - in this case - meteor strikes. You had to make the choice between investing scarce resources (money) and your attention (time) on which of the three mitigation factors to focus on - all the while trying to ensure that the settlement kept developing. There was a continual trade off between allocating resources to 'research' the meteor, improve the resilience to the impacts or two insure against damage.
Risk preparation affected development both immediately - as you spent money on insurance - you had less money to upgrade your 'pods' and long term - when you were researching the meteors you couldn't invest in increasing the resilience of the settlements.
