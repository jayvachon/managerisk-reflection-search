The risk horizon was very interesting but was a mysterious game. No instructions were available rather it was told that the instructions are embedded in the game and one has to learn by hit and trial method. The symbols used for buildings / constructions were unusual and there was no indication / instruction how to raise the level of the constructed area. Moreover, I could not understand how the money in my wealth pool is increasing and with what mechanism till I submitted my assignment.

I also could not understand the mechanism of research its benefits and the cost I paid for it. I tried many tradeoffs to complete the game and to understand the risk management mechanism but miserably failed to cross level III. I could not understand how my investment in development of the community increased my wealth pool. Despite of the fact that I had a lot of money in my wealth pool and lot of development on ground and Insurance of different levels, I never felt short of money to cover the damage caused by the comets but these could not help me to reach level III. Although I played the game many times with different combinations of protection, insurance, research andvarious level of developments but could not understand the risk management mechanism of the game. I purchased higher insurance, I constructed pods in almost all of the area available, I tried to enter research area, I protected the development in different combinations and in different attempts but could not cross level III.

For developing a new version of the game I would add the mechanism of increasing the wealth pool and cost of each action like construction and it’s up gradations, cost of damage according to percentage. How many comets are expected? There would be not only damage in development work but also loss of jobs and lives during shocks, whose loss is also to be included. If all these parameters are given before the start of the game or side by side it will show your capacity to deal with risk management with your knowledge and resources available.

In the end it will be interesting to note that without buying any kind of insurance or spending on research you can go to level III of the game just by investing in development and raising the level of protection and any one can do that.
