Therefore, from this game I learned following two elements:

1.      After establishing the context, the next step in the process of managing risk is to identify potential risks. Risks are about events that, when triggered, cause problems or benefits. Hence, risk identification can start with the source of our problems and those of our competitors (benefit), or with the problem itself. Once risks have been identified, they must then be assessed as to their potential severity of impact and to the probability of occurrence. These quantities can be either simple to measure, in the case of the value of a lost building, or impossible to know for sure in the case of the probability of an unlikely event occurring.

2.       Risk management regarding natural disasters : It is important to assess risk in regard to natural disasters like floods caused by comet like in Alora community, and so on. Outcomes of natural disaster risk assessment are valuable when considering future repair costs,
