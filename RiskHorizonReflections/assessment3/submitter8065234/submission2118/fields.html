My Game Reflection  :

The role of risk management in the game, as I saw it, was to show the importance of risk management for healthy development of the stated community. Better preparation, longer researching and comparable protection and insurance levels, helped to defend the damaging effects of waves from comets on the one hand and tested the ability of the community for faster repair and recovery when the level of protection did not cope with the severity of the damage on the other hand, which I thought were reflections of the situation in the real world.

To be better prepared and minimize the risk of waves from comets as shocks and to recover the community faster, I applied the effective risk management components of research/Knowledge, protection and insurance. In so doing, it was not an easy choice given the limited resources of time and money. While research gave me a better insight about the severity and probability of the shock from comets and hence more time for preparation, the cost for protection and insurance was challenging as it had a direct implication on the development efforts. Too much emphasis on preparation left fewer resources for development of the community and too much emphasis on development left the later without protection. So, a balance between the two was a necessary step  I took during the game.

When I played the game the first time, I felt nervous for fear that I might not control the situation at the screen. I even felt that a person better on games might progress well ahead without understanding the concept of risk management. Plus, at the first play, I didn’t give much attention to research which I later realized the importance of researching the horizon for better preparation for future shocks and took action afterwards.

The most important lessons, I believe, I take from playing this game are;  the importance of balance in deploying resources for preparation for risk management and pursuing development opportunities, the importance of knowledge or research for better preparation for future shocks, and the importance of practice, as was the case in the game,of the scientific approaches of effective risk management tools.

In the mean time, if I had to suggest an improvement on the game, it would be the high probability of comets as shocks. For the game to represent the case on the real world, probably in my area, I thought, comets should appear less often and less severe , otherwise, we wouldn't be survived at any level of preparation.
