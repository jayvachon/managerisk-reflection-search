Risk management can be a powerful instrument for development.Risk is everywhere. While risks in some areas and 
some regions have diminished in recent years—in part because of improved macroeconomic and financial management and better preventive health care in developing countries—people in developing and developed countries alike continue to face a multitude of risks.Failure to prevent and prepare for risk can have tragic consequences—often leading to widespread loss of life. Risk management help in preventing that consequences or atleast somewhat make us prepare for it . We read that in our RESOURCE material. 

In this game RISK MANAGEMENT was an important factor. The goal of our game was to fill the meter fast while protecting the pods from comets and at the same time doing research and providing insurance.We must create a balance between growing our community and preparing for these potential shocks.While protecting our land we have to let go of our meter so as to protect our selves from the upcoming comets. We have to take a RISK to protect our pods. In between doing so we have to carry out our research while insuring ourselves against the odds.By obtaining insurance, pods damaged from flooding will be quicker and cheaper to rebuild. Insurance also recovers lost development time after you your pods are repaired! Making multiple connections was a alternative of protecting ourselves after calamity has happened and we can achieve so by doing more and more research. If the comet does not hit, then the opportunity cost is the time that we spend on research than developing our community. Each action will cost us precious resources!
