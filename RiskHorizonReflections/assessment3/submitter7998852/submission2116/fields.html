On the game the risk management was key for to reach the top levels, by administrating tools like research, insurance and protection, combining them in the best way as possible for to prevent and manage the risk of the comet clash.

At first my strategy was to invest the most on the buidings construction and protection, with almost not investment on research and just very little on insurance,  but I kept loosing even when my city was large developed not because a comet impact but because of  the bar of development was not on the mark, I really didn´t understand why at all, so I think  the absent of research looking the comets was the problem and researching was then a must, I noticed the left bar grew faster with research, then in my final game I did strong inversion on protection and research, while keeping the buildings at high growing rate updating them as soon as I could but only few at a time and not all at once as I was pretending at the beginning. I eventually bought insurance at 4th level but it was not necessary because really highs levels of protection were achieved. In conclusion the best combination for me was to invest strong on protection and research for to avoid damage, and keep the city buildings updating.

That shows to me that in real life is very desirable to do research and adquire enough knowledge for to prevent, control and content the problem, generating the correct strategy of protection with that information, a city with no research and no idea of prevention strategies is in high risk and can be easily damaged, to invest on prevention also decrease the need of insurance to a second place.

There are floods on my city but there are also strong earthquakes and they happen really often,  which I believe could be also used for representing risk on games like this, because we also need to improve our knowledge, build better and stronger buildings and be prepared with insurance and emergency plans when this happens.  Maybe it could be an interesting idea to make the game more complex by adding several types of risk that can happen at any time and not only comets, then insurance will be more useful than it actually is in the present version of the game.
