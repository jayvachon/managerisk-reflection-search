Risk management plays a vital role in the game. Understanding the concept of Risk management is the condition precedent before any participant in this online program can effectively appreciate and explore the world of challenges imbued in this risk horizon game. In a nutshell, risk management is the knowledge hub with which a player can juggle the three laminator powers in the horizon game. To start with, the falling comets representing different shocks are of divergent sizes. I utilized the concept of preparation to ascertain the target shocks which I kept in focus to harness the power of research. By so doing, I had ample knowledge of “the chances of hit” of the 5 Meteteors (shocks) that prompted.

The knowledge of these “chances of hit” placed me on my guard as regards protection which I constantly upgraded as the chances of hit drew closer and it had a positive effect. I traded off insurance in the exercise as I relied basically on knowledge and protection.      

Preparation for risk positively affected development in the game (both immediately and after) through the systematic building of pods which created community connections which assisted people when shocks came. For example, the connection of the pods in the game when floods crystalized.      

I attempted the game severally before being able to reach stage two. In the earlier attempts, I deployed research, protection and insurance in different proportions but never recorded any meaningful result. For me to get to the stage two, insurance was excluded to pave way for research and protection. The key things I learnt from the game is that community affiliation can fast track a long term protection and insurance. This is because connected neighbours, friends and well-wishers can always rally round each other in the event of a shock crystalizing. Furthermore, the game brought to bear the nexus amongst the three concepts of knowledge, protection and insurance.

If I am given an opportunity to re-program this game and tailor it to serve my immediate environment, I will incorporate the following into the game: first, heavy rainfall will be introduced followed by insufficient or blocked drainage channels. This will have an effect of flood ravaging the city which today in my country destroys lives and properties and no remedial step is being taken to address same. The authorities have the knowledge of what to do but have neglected to do same, the citizens can subscribe to insurance by insuring their lives and properties against destruction by flood for the ultimate effect of achieving protection. Finally, the people can pull resources together in conjunction with foreign donor agencies to address the flooding issues.
