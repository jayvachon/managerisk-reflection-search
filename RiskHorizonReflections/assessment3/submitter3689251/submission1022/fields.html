I think the goal of this game is about how to balance between the development and disaster you may face in the game. In other word, this is a sustainable development game. This game mainly focuses on preparation the risk, balance three factor : knowledge – protection - insurance

-          Knowledege :

o   how to play the game : the function of each buton, each activity

o   research the coming comet : chance of the comet hitting, estimate the time until impact, the severity of the comet.

o   Knowledge from the previous play.

o   Analyse the cost – benefit when do research – protection – purchase insurance.

-          Protection :

o   the more high level, the more it costs

o   prevent the pods from being damaged

o   Raise the island to higher level.

o   Always remain the minimum of the money which need to purchase insurance, recover the pods if comet hit the planet.

o   Protection will cost immediately, but it’s cost is lower than the benefits.

-          Insurance :

o   recovers lost development

o   reduce damaged from flooding

o   If the severity of the comet is lager than the protection level, then I would purchase the highest insurance level and vise versa.

The first time I play, it makes me quite confused because I suffer much damage from the comet, lost the development, I look like that I can not reach the finish level. the pressure of development ( time and status of the development ) makes me only concern, focus on building and upgrading activities, less focus on protection. if there are some comet hit the planet, then the shock wave will destroys the pods, erode the development, it cost me a lot to recover the pods and i never can reach the finished level.

But playing again and again, this helps me me change my strategy, improve my knowledge: How to play, how to protect my development, my award? Whether should purchase an insurance or not? How much money should i remain to coping with the risk, recover the pods from damage, analyse cost-benefit immediately,… I am now not just focus on development but also the protection too. One thing  I have learned form the game is that if you don’t put effort on protection activities enough and just only concern the insurance, you  also will bear the lost, damage from the shocks, suffer the negative side of the risk that you confront.

I think we live in an uncertain world, we dont know what we will face and what shocks we might meet. So it would be better if we manage risk, prepare for the negative side of the risk and take advantage to the positive side of risk that will come to us, not only in my own life but also in any business, affairs.
