I have been playing several strategy games related to issues such as urbanization like My Country 2020 and Megapolis  and also other environmental protection simulation. Those games have complex rules to complexly reflect the reality as much as possible. However Risk Horizone Game I played is much different. Despite simple rule, this game reflects very strategic and realistic simulation regarding to risk management.

The game reflects the disguised of risk in everyday life that we had ignored. The risk in the game is the protect the building from the meteor which caused the wave the damage the construction. There is trade-off between resource from development and resource for risk management.

When I first played this game, I thought the rule is too easy to follow and the risk was so easy to handle so I didn’t need to spend much effort on this game. At the first game play, I didn’t understand much about the rule of protection, assurance, researches and communication that I needed to re-read the instruction several times before I played again.  As I played, I focused much on development on assets and totally forgot about the resource and protection or strategy to reduce impact of risk. As result, my construction was damaged for several times. After several games, I focused more on protection, assurance and research tools so I could pass level 2 of the game. Therefore, I feel that I learned and enjoyed the game more than before.

Despite several flaws the game can attach players the knowledge learned and also reflect the decision to make in reality as they play. The drawback of the game is that there is only one types of risk that is easily predictable. As the reality, there are many type of risks and hard to predict. Plus, the tools they used are too simple that they are just clicked. It is not to make any more effort in finding more solution rather than randomly click on each tools and hope for the best. However, the best part of the game is to be proactive in risk protection and be familiar with risk. Regarding to solution for risk management there are two parts which are protection and mitigation. Protection include research for understanding the risk, protection and assurance, while mitigation is too communication. As I believe, the most important part of the game is to make decision at each situation given. When the decision is made well, the implementation will be likely to be better manage the risk.

The modified version of the game that I suggest is to make it multiplayers. There most important tool of risk management in my countries is pooling resource and risk communication. No one can cope risk alone and it is very costly. Pooling resource and help each other one at the time is very important. They need to work corporately in the community.

In Conclusion, playing this game make me more proactive in making decision which prepare me better for risk management in real life rather than took risk for granted and did not manage my resource effectively for risk management.
