In the game, the role of risk management is keeping the development of the beautiful world Alora to meet the milestone within time limit. There are many comets can hit Alora causing damages and floods. I made choices between three of Luminatorâ€™s powers, such as Research, Protection and Insurance. Certainly, each action will cost me precious resource. Protection will slow down short term development immediately, but this long term protection will prevent my pods from being damaged when hit by the comets depending on protection level I had. Research will have great effect right before the comet hits to create community connection and to boost protection. Especially for more severe comets, I will have more time to prepare with more research. Finally, insurance will affect after the hitting of the comets. By purchasing insurance, the cost to rebuild pods damaged from flooding will be quicker and cheaper. Insurance also recovers lost development time after my pods are repaired.

I played the game more than once. I cannot meet the development milestone at the first time, so I tried to build more pods and upgraded them on the second time. At this time, I can see my development and resources increased a lot. However, I failed again because I forgot to obtain insurance at this second time. After hitting by the comets, I cost me a great number of resources, so I am lack of money to build new pods to catch up development. For the third time, I forgot to increase the Protection level so the speed of development cannot meet the deadline. In my opinion, in order to succeed with the game, players have to be as quick as possible in making choices, saving every second and to find the perfect balance between spending and investment on Research, Protection and Insurance powers.

Currently, players can only do one job at one time. I can only choose Research or Protection. If I am tasked with developing a new version of the game, I will add one feature that players can do multi tasks. For example, players can do Research for several comets and increase Protection level at the same time within available resources. My new feature to the game will better represent the risk management choices that are close to the real world and really matter in the place I live. Some policies can be made one by one, but some policies can be proceeded at the same time.
