MY REFLECTIONS FROM THE RISK HORIZON GAME

The role of the risk management in the game is to develop and protect the community against all obstacles. By building and upgrading the pods to increase the rate of development.

 

The choice I made was to stand to build the community and putting all protective measures in place to balance the growth and prepare for potential shocks.

To protect and creating a healthy community despite the waves that hinder the development and create a huminator lagacy. 

 

The trade-off I confronted in terms of knowledge, preparation and Insurance was to let go of my resources in order to again.

In terms of knowledge I was able to forecast the next hit, In terms of preparation, I was able to create connections and with Insurances I was able to repair my damage pods.

 

Preparation generally allows me to learn the chances of protecting the community immediately and developing the community after some time.   

 

What I did differently after the first time I played the game was to study the chance of the comet stocking the earth, I was able to predict and took the advantage to create connection to boost my protection.

I was also able to protect against the waves by raising the Island in order to protect the pods from damage.

Besides that, I also took advantage of the Insurance to quickly repair my damage pods.
