Risk Horizon is a very interesting and well developed interactive game, even my nephew of 9 years like it, and he was very interested of playing it. The role of risk management was crucial at the game; shown risk as the meteorites was a good analogy to real life where the analysis (research) is very important to have better information of the possible risk, its impact (severity) and possible consequences. We must find equilibrium of the resources destiny to knowledge, protection and insurance. In the game preparation for risk affect almost immediately to the level of development, in real life it could take more time but its importance is undeniable.  The main thing I have learned of playing the game is that future is unknown so we must prepare the most to it, doing research of the develop of the circumstances (in this case of risk and opportunities situation), learning from before (like making mistakes involved in the experience of playing the game like building unnecessary pod or desperate on evolution) and developing knowledge (practical and theoretical) which helps facing different types of risk situations. Finally if I’m asked to add something to better represent the risk management in real life to the game, I would try to add something that represents the resources imply into development, like labor, land, technology and capital involved in the creation of the pots: Where are these resources take of? How much do we have of them? And what are the chances of use them properly?
