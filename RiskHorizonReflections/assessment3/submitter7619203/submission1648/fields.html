The challenge was to make very quick and fundamental decision in order to mitigate and avoid as much as possible the impact. It was necessary to manage a budget necessary to acquire insurance in order to get insured in case of impact and damage. As well with the available budget it could be possible to protect the existing infrastructure and repair the damaged ones. After some time it was clearer the potential areas where the meteorites could hit the ground, so the rehabilitation areas were chosen in area with limited probability of damage. N this case rehabilitation works had less costs and less burden in the communities.
