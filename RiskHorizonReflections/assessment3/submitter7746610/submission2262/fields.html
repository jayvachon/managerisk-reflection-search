The main role of the game for risk management is about getting knowledge to prepare for uncertainties like natural disasters, flooding, etc. Besides getting knowledge you had to build infrastructure and improve them for better resilience.

Also you had to balance between protection and insurance, to protect the community, to recover faster than previous shocks and to improve all.

In terms of knowledge, protection, and insurance I made choices like first build pods, then I made some savings to get better protection, when problems occurred, like the first meteor hit, I had to get knowledge about it, as I gained more, I had more money to improve protection, and when I had to balance between protection and uncertainty I had to buy some insurance, to cover the losses I couldn’t protect.

As I was shocked, I had no damages, so I could improve buildings to make stronger the community and to build better resilience to problems. But then as I advanced in levels, I learn that problems where bigger and many more than before, so I had to gain knowledge again, to balance between protection and insurance, but also to improve infrastructure to develop faster and as equal as I could for the community.

Preparation for risks affects development in a way that if you don’t prepare for risk, than the shock is bigger so you have to spend more money, time, less or not resilience to problems, and most important is that you lack in terms of time.

I played the game like seven times, I learn that is very important to gain knowledge about risks you can have, this makes you prepare better for them, and when you cannot protect from something very unknown in terms of probability, you have to acquire some insurance, to help you in losses in cases of shocks, or to obtain profits if you did a good preparation and the risk turns into an opportunity.
