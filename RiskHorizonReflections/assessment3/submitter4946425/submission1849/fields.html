The role of Risk Management in the game is

1.    To provide a methodology to identify and analyze the financial impact of loss in Alora Island

  1. To examine the use of realistic and cost-effective opportunities in order to prevent natural disasters (Prevention for comets)
  2. To prepare insurance budgets to prevent future damage due to cost overruns.

4.    To realize that  Risk Managemnt cannot be successful without the assistance of other groups or communities.

5.    Allow the development of populations in a secure way

 

What choices did you make and what trade-offs did you confront in terms of knowledge, protection, and insurance?

At first, whenever the chances of damage were high, I endeavored to save some wealth to ensure the island (buy insurance). In this way, I was assured that no damage (quite probable) could let me without resources to further encourage the development

Secondly, referring to knowledge, I tried to look at least 3 times the comet and its associated data in order to equal protection with the severity.

Finally, once achieved levels of safety and security, I tried to develop the city, leaving reserves of wealth for possible accidents.

The preparation for the risk definitely affected the development of the city of Alora. Thus, it was necessary to save resources of wealth in order to be prepared for future repairs after accidents instead of using them to develop the city.

In addition, also a large amount of wealth was used to buy the insurance as well as to protect the city (it was necessary to leave always sa reservation forfuture possible increases of protection)

 In my case, I've played the game several times.

The first time I was very protectionist, ie, I used many resources to assure the city(as purchasing the most expensive insurances), to increase protection ... and fewer resources to develop the city and increase the level of development in the pods, which was a mistake because it kept me from beat levels.
