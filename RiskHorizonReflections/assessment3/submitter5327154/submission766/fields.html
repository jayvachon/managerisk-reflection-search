1. The risk game involved the building and protecting of an Island from comets which seek to destroy the pods on the planet (a systemic risk). Risk management played a very crucial role in this game. Risk management is the process of confronting risks, preparing for them, and coping with their effects. In order to protect the Island from the comets, it was imperative that adequate risk management procedures were put in place. In order to protect the island from the comets, there were three key risk management tools available on the game.

·        Research- gaining knowledge about the comets was important. The acquisition of this knowledge reduced the severity of the attacks. It could be inferred that with the more knowledge of a risk, we have better tools to either prevent them from occurring or mitigate the effects when they do. The more time spent on research also helped reduce the damage caused by the comet as I was able to receive more time to create community connections which helped boost my protection.

·        Protection- protecting the island from the waves was very important. When the comet hit the protection available helped prevent damage to the pods, which in turn saved a lot of money which would have been spent on repairs.

·        Insurance- by obtaining insurance I was able to save money and time. The insurance purchased ensured that the pods damaged from flooding were quicker and cheaper to rebuild. It also ensured that development time lost was recovered after the pods were repaired.

The three risk management tools were all important, but I discovered that research served me best in the protection of the Island. I spent more time on research than on increasing my protection level. I spent time on research while the community kept accumulating enough money which aided in its development.

The preparation for the risk affected the development of the community immediately. Through research community connections were made and this immediately helped reduce the damage of the disaster on the community. The severity of the disaster was also reduced through research. The insurance purchased helped save some money, and the protection of the pod reduced the damage on the pods.

2.I played the game more than once, and I discovered that the choice of risk management tool used had a huge impact on the outcome of the game. All risk management tools available were very necessary. However I discovered that the use of research was the most effective to the overall protection of the Island.

The second time I played the game, I spent more time on research than on protection, and this made all the difference.
