Dear Team,

First of all, I need to mention it was really hard job. Here is small virtual place that took me more than two hours to achieve 4th level, learn how to create, protect and develop it. You can imagine how many mistakes I had to admit. But in a real life for sure we have no room for error. The main role of risk management in the game in my case was to protect my village and develop it, I almost didn’t use insurance. Sometimes the affection from preparation was observed immediately, sometimes after some time. 

First time I have built all homes, but it became very complicated to protect them, and I decided to build just 3-4 to develop it and then began to build others. Also it is very important to do research about comets because sometimes you see it is coming and buy insurance use protection but in reality it will not offend damages. What it comes to insurance, if you are going to use it do it later after 3rd level for example. 

The main things I learned from the game, be careful with details, use all information and only necessary points that make a sense now. Be always on guard. 
Maybe in a new version I would add other cases as financial, I do not mean financial strategy for government or company, it can be made easy, such own financial strategy, made some decisions and then learn would you lost all your money, assets or safely outlive financial crisis.
