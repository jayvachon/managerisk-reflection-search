1)    The role of risk management is to protect from the effects of uncertainty and it is the same case in the game risk horizon. Any form of development is backed by risk and shielding from it is the role and responsibility of risk management. Similarly the three ways of risk management in the game, which are, POWER OF RESEARCH, which gave knowledge about every comet, their probability of hitting the island and the severity. POWER OF PROTECTION, which reduced the magnitude of negativity from comet hits and increased the gain from positive effects and also the POWER OF INSURANCE, which helped to shift the cost to externalities and save the resources for better use. All these three ways ensured that the development made by creating pods is least destructed every time a comet hits the island.

To maintain a balance between the cost of development and cost of risk management is tough and the cost of risk management involved was the trade-off faced every time a comet hit the island and increased my cost of repairing the pods

Every time I prepared for development I had to ensure allotment of a considerable amount in protecting the pods by either raising the island or purchasing insurance. As a result of ensuring more protection I had to give away to development often as costs involved was high for both development and protection.Lack of fund to invest affected immediate development.

 

2)    I played the game more than once. In the first round I faced the difficulty of maintaining a balance between the cost to develop pods and cost of risk management. Because of which I took long time to develop and lost considerable amount of time in managing all together. But in the next round I understood how to manage both the cost and time so that I develop pods quickly along with protecting those from the risk of being hit by comets.

The main learning from the game which I had is to balance the cost of development and cost of risk management within the stipulated time.
