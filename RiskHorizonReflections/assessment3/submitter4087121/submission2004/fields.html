Game reflection

Although this game has a quite simple concept, it represents the main principles of effective risk management: a pool of resources (your financial pool), grade of development (the development of your village), protection (the level of the stone platform), insurance (it covers damage after a comet hits), research (for a small price you can know the severance and the probability of a hit) and social connections (they help after a hit; unfortunately, this was the only power which functioned in a quite weird way). It looks very simple, but do not be lighthead. In order to succeed, you have to find a sound balance using all your resources efficiently without wasting them and to access the risks (comets and consequences of a hit) on time. Well, this game is a good simplified simulation of a risk management situation.   

 

My first lesson from this game was that knowledge helps avoiding risks. I started to play the game without reading the rules and could reach only level 3. I could succeed better after reading them. In some sense, it could also be an example of risk management: you have to have knowledge to proceed and to avoid risks and to know how to benefit from advantages you have.  

 

Furthermore, the game was one more to prove that you do not need to achieve  always the best score to succeed, but you have to be constant. You do not need to build the whole village in the first round, although you could. More houses would mean more expenses. You can complete building your village in following rounds. Besides, the more developed your village is, the more you pay for upgrading it and repairs after a hit. You have to think twice about an efficient use of your resources (money pool). Moreover, the insurance was very useful. I used only 75%-insurance and twice 50% insurances. Without them, it is difficult to cover all your losses.

 

Nevertheless, the representation of the social connections is not transparent. It could be just a technical problem, but the flashes moved quite sporadically. Maybe, the idea was to show uncontrollable social connections, I still would improve the representation and the functioning of this “power” in the game.

 

To sum up, this game is a good simulation for beginners to have, and even to “play”, a world leader and to learn main principles of risk management in practice.
