The game is not only fun but it help realize and evaluate alternatives at different scenarios. That is, one have the choice of protection, research, insurance and developments. The game puts the player on the spot, where he has to continuously choose at what point of the decision space he locates himself. The decision space has the following dimensions: Development, Insurance, Protection and Research, none of which is preferred to the others.
