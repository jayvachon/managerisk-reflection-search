Role Of Risk Management in Game

 

In game we have to use a balance between research, insurance and protection. It gave us practical simulation of how to give attention to which thing most.

 

Strategy used by me in game

Level 1 –my whole attention was on development and protection I did not used insurance since the level of risk was protected by in in advance.

Level-2 – in this level again my particular attention was on research and development since the problem could be mitigated by spending time on protection.

Level-3- again I used the same strategy but this time I was hit by Metroid it decreased my development level but I managed to get it.

Level 4 -I purchased insurance this time and spend less money on protection since I was ensured my development does not go down even after getting   attacked by Metroid. For getting more time for mitigation I spend time on research.

Level 5- same strategy matching up development with level spending money on protection as well as insurance.

Level 6 cannot complete it.

 

If I play next time I will send more on protection less on insurance.

I understand how it is good to be prepared for disaster by all ways i.e. protection as well as insurance.

 

Change I will propose to better represent the risk management choices that matter in the place I live will be to make it a hilly terrain so that ability to take decision depending on location of settlement also effect so that people could understand that development is necessary but in a scientifically planned way.
