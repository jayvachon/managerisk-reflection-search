Question 1

There is a role for Risk Management in the Game because I took Risk with the Possibility of Loss, which advance me when I Win and retard me when I Lose and I also utilized Opportunity with Possibility of Gain, which advance me when I Win and retard me when I Lose. Thus the Balance between Research to gain information on Severity of Hit and Chance of Hit; the Purchase of Insurance and the Building of Protection, all of which have Costs, is Key to whether the Risks I take or the Opportunities I pursue take me from One Level to another in the Game or not.

The more Protection and Research I do to prepare for Risk, the less Development I can do, hence need for Balance mentioned above.

Question 2
