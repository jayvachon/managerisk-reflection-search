1) Risk management in this game manifests in two ways one is building resilience (the ability to recover from sea waves generated by the comets); the second is building prosperity (being able to use money to build and upgrade plots).

The key point here is being able to manage both time and money (resources).

What I really focused on was knowledge and tried to use protection and insurance minimum time. This technique saved me a lot money in which I built more plots and upgraded them.

This gave the opportunity to have a good development is some time.

2) As one plays more his experience grows; the same as real life risk management where time after time knowledge and different techniques to attack the risk is occurred.

 If I played the game I would invest more in knowledge rather than protection and insurance in the early stages.

I have learned from the game that managing resources (time and money) are very important.

3)  The change I would propose would be introducing more than one risk, which one can encounter in real life. This would be challenging and more life realistic where good risk management practice is needed to direct the resources to the right time and right place.
