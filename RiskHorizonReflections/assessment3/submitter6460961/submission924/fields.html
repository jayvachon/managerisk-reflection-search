Upon seeing the game for the first time, I felt disappointed to find a cartoonish and childish game. The game reminded me of the online games I used to play when I was around 10’s. Game is designed very amateurly for sure but later I realized that this game was not necessarily created to entertain me.

Despite its very low graphical quality, game really delivers its real purpose. It perfectly illustrates the importance and also the challenges of risk management. One can’t afford to pay too much for insurance and also can’t afford to let things go without any insurance. This dilemma is well established in the game. Like in the real world, chance is also a part of the game which makes the game more realistic. Furthermore researches about risks really help during the times of contingency as in the real world.The risks however are caricatured as they are portrayed as asteroid falling on planet. That might maybe justify itself considering that this game is created also for children and requires to be not too much complex.

Overall I think the game serves well to its purpose of creation. It could be done with better graphics but in the end it is quite good.
