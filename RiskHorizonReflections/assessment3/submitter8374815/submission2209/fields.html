Answer to second question

First time I have made the following mistakes.

1.     Rapid heavy investment in the initial phase by building  multiple pods horizontally. 

2.     Ignoring  maintaining severity levels of protection according to the severity of  comets as observed in Research. 

3.     Missing  white mark at the top of  development bar indicating required level for development for transfer to the next level. 

4.     Ignored the level of   protection required  against falling comets at different levels of development.

5.     Heavily investment in  insurance in the initial levels.

6.     Spending less time Research of comets thereby losing the reward of  extra time that is required for making  community connections in rescue and relief operations when comets hit the community. 

 I have tried to avoid repeating  those mistakes in later attempts. .

 Lessons learnt from the game. 

Avoiding a risk is not a solution.

Sustainable development is only possible when we are prepared for risk management.

Positive risks could be exploited for boosting economy.
