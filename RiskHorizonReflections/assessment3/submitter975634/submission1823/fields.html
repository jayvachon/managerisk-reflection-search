Risk management has a core role it this game. You can build and develop your own planet, you can make researches which help you to evaluate and predict your risks, you can insure and protect your community. After each level you get detailed explanation of what happened and it helps a lot. Some tools gave you immediate result but some stays with you for longer period of time.
I played this game many times. My curiosity and 'And what if I ...?' question made me play this game over and over again.
I tried to build different communities.
I built communities of 9, 4 and 2 members. My try to develop only 1 member was absolutely not successful. So my key learning point #1 is it's better to play in a team and live in a community. In real life it means support, help and speed while you are preparing for the future risks or fixing the consequences.
I tried to add it all at the same time and one by one, and I found out that in this game it's better to add it one by one. In this case you have an opportunity to build and develop the community. I had a feeling that I'm leading my community to the better future. And key learning point #2 is don't try to get everything at the same time. Make baby steps and don't rush things.
If we are talking about different insurance plans than I can say that I've tried them all too. I tried to buy only one plan but also I tried to buy new ones during the game. And of course I tried to play without it just using researches as a crazy. Key learning point #3 it's possible to live without any insurance but you must be VERY careful. You have to be aware of every single thing around you. Key learning point #4 don't think that the best insurance plan will save your life. No, it's not going to be like that. That money will help you to rebuild everything after collapse but it will not make any change without good researches and innovations implementation.
