                The main role of risk management in the game was about making the decision when to invest in improving your colony and when to invest in building up your defenses. There is an ongoing threat of comets and you have to balance your investment in whether you will invest on your colony, protection or insurance. These tradeoffs are the key to successfully building up your colony through mitigating the risks of comets.

                When I was playing I realized that I was investing too much on insurance and instead I decided to build up my defenses early for the second time. This helped me protect my colony from many comets and I had more resources to develop my colony. The balance between protection and pods was the key for me to get to level 5. I only needed insurance once through all 5 levels. I learned that there are two types of risks: ones that you can avoid and ones that you can’t. However, even with the risks that you can’t avoid, it is still possible to mitigate them in order to reduce their effects. In addition, I also got to witness how insurance can be a lifesaver in desperate situations.

                I would add more investment options on higher levels. This would help the game be more interesting and prove how different options pay out at the end. Also, there should be a numeric label beside the progress bar at the left hand side. It is too ambigious. This would also help us understand how pods contribute to progress and see how upgrading the pods help the progress bar. I also didn’t find an explanation about whether the progress bar is still filled if the pod is being upgraded or not. This might help strategically when you are running out of time and you need the progress bar to fill up.
