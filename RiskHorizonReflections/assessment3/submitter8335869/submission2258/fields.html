Key elements of the game.

Knowledge

Knowing what the impact of each shock is vital on the preparation. Investing in knowing the effects represents only a fraction of the money speculating on the costs of the shocks or overprotecting (which also slows down the development).

 

Protection

Protection is never enough, and the level of protection decreases on every impact, so a constant investment on protection has to be made. Protection also minimizes the effects of the shocks. Investing on protection do not sound like a very a good idea because it freezes development which is necessary to advance to the next level, never the less is vital to keep buildings from being over harmed and therefore investing more money on reconstruction.  

 

Insurance

Without spending on insurance it is impossible to succeed in the game. Without it expenses of reconstruction go sky high and the money available is not closely enough to reconstruct all the buildings after each shock. Eventually, such as spending on knowledge, insurance pays for itself and result a very cheap decision.

 

To define which of the tools mentioned previously on the essay is more important is impossible. While I at first thought that insurance was the most important I kept paying the most expensive one, even if the level didn’t have meteorites with a lot of harm or probability to hit. The same happened to me with the protection. I kept spending on protection and not achieving the goal because time was not enough. I believe that the most important learning from this exercise is that decisions have to be made ad hoc to each one of the crisis, integrating all the tools available and giving for granted that all the meteorites will impact, and the most important, investing wisely the money to reduce the impacts of each shock. The role of risk management is the fundamental idea of the whole exercise. The game consists on calculating how much will the impacts will affect the village and which tool has the higher benefit for its cost.
