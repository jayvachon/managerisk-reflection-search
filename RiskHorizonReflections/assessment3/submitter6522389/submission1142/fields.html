MY GAME REFLECTION 

 The concepts of risk and disaster are associated with a disorder and chaos involving what is established in its normal order very obvious reality. These situations are very harmful to the people especially those in need.

In doing so, the paradox remains that the risk is inevitable for humanity, hence the need for countries and organizations to establish an effective plan and goal to control seizures. This plan should be a priority of development policies implemented in the State. Research into risk therefore facilitates the passage of expertise to the decision to avoid social chaos often observed and very detrimental to the integrity and human dignity.

In this game, the role of risk management has been taking the necessary precautions to prevent their falling comets do not cause waves that hinder development on my territory (Alora). In doing so, I got first information about the characteristics of each obstacle in terms of its severity, my chances of avoiding it. This analysis allowed me to know the kind of power to deploy between the'' Search'', the'' protection'' and'' insurance''; knowing that each deployed strategy may cost me a lot of resources.

Strategy'' Search'' allowed me to better inform me on comets to know what type of protection deployed. Strategy'' protection'' has allowed me to avoid the damage or reduce their intensity if they managed to reach my territory. On the strategy of'' insurance'', it allowed me to repair the damage caused by obstacles in my territory and which could reduce the development time lost.

My first experience with this game has not been a success. Later, I understood that it was very early advice about the obstacles, develop defense mechanisms and create non-vulnerable areas. '' Zero’’ risk does not exist, so I sometimes opted for insurance to repair the damage in case of attack.

For the improvement of means of protection, I suggest that there are techniques for destroying threats before they reach my territory. I also suggest that there are early warning systems to evacuate the people who are in risk zones. It will also raise awareness and inform them precautions taken in case of attack.

These precautions will reduce damage in case of attack on human and material plans. During the post-attack period, it must also help those affected to recover with dignity and rebuild their future populations. It was a very instructive game and it allowed me to better understand the concept of risk management.

It allowed me to learn to develop strategies for research, prevention and adaptation.
