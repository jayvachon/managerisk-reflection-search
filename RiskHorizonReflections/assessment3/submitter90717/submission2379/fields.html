[1]In this game we balance risk management and healthy development.

 We should develop our lands and protect it against Meteors.

We can use insurance in 3 mode (33%,50% and 70%) ,option to protect lands against meteors waves and also  research  on risks(if we do more research we have more chance to decrease damages.)

Each risk has some information such as chance of hit and severity  , risk management role is to control insurance , research and protection to reduce risks damages and develop lands.

We should prepare for risks with using of protection mode , insurance , and level up our lands . we should also  research on meteors to have chances to reduce damages when coping with it and if meteors hit our lands we should repair them as soon as possible to back levels again.

Preparation for risks with insuring help us after some time , when lots of high risks meteors come in sequences  and more research and using wave protection help development at hit moment.

[2] I played this game 4 times and reached level 5 at first try I did small research and used 70% insurance and reached level 2 , at next tries I start with 50% wave protection and looked at chance of hit and severity and did large research on high risk meteors and tried to level up lands as much as possible in lower levels .The main things I learned from this game is that increase  research and knowledge  is one of the best ways that can make good situation on coping and preparation with risks.
