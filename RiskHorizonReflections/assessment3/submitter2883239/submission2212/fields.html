Alora, an imaginary island is prone to destruction form meteor shower. As Luminator I have the task of its development by way of building and upgrading pods at every level. In addition to this I am also responsible for paving the way for the least damage if a meteor strike is certain. The task of protecting Alora and developing it at the same time is achieved by the following ways:

·         Building pods and upgrading them subject to the availability of money, the level of development to be achieved and time constraint

·         Investing in insurance in the right amount. It is necessary to identify the right amount because of the limited availability of money. 

·         Research which is gathered by way of observation

·         Protection by way of raising Alora to a optimum height

·         Networking in the event of a strike

·         Building resilience to withstand a disaster.

Choices that were made were subject to factors like severity of strike, chance of hit etc. My choices were customised according to factors like time, development, research, money. I played through all the levels so the choices that were recurrent were upgrading of pods, raising the level of Alora, research. Speaking of trade-off, the one that strikes my mind is insurance which I used only once. Others would be lack of optimum research when the meteor was moving very fast. I always chose raising the level of Alora as means of protection. This affected development as I was constantly using up the money I was entitled to use. Moreover, the research also got hampered during this time.

As I mentioned that I have completed all the levels. In the first attempt I made it till level 4. Here I was using up the money on insurance, so I couldn’t upgrade the pods hence the development goal was not achieved. In the next attempt I stopped investing in the insurance which I used only in level 6. In this whole attempt I focused on upgrading the pods at the same time I maintained an optimum height of the island to avert damage caused. With this strategy I sailed across quite easily.

The game conveyed the idea of risk management in a very simple way. Introspectively, I learned that money which is a medium of exchange; is of extreme importance and at the same time scarce and negligent use of it can be a massive obstacle to development. Once I was convinced that I was moving with the required pace for development I used my time in protection of Alora. Time management is of equal importance. All the development process has to in tune with time available otherwise all the hard work will go in vain.

 Everything that I learned by playing the game has taught me life lessons which I can apply in my daily life. Time is the most important factor for me and progress has to be made for self development. Achievement of short term goals paves the way for the bigger picture. Social connections can be of great help in times of need. Therefore having support of friends, neighbours is a must. Keeping in touch with the current affairs is a great necessity as it allows the perception of an eminent danger lurking in the horizon. Money is also an important factor which makes it possible for attainment of the necessities of life.
