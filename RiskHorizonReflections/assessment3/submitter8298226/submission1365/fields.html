This game conveys the message that if you manage risk in good manner you will reduce damages at minimum level. Every action in economic environment is decided based on cost-benefit comparison: (i) you pay money to buy insurance you will get the protection; (ii) You built up more spot you have to pay money but you will get stronger spot; (iii) If you click early on comics you will lost your time in doing research but you get more time to connect community and you get more time to built up for yourself the early alarming system and more time for reparation shield to cope with risk; (iv) if you spend money on upgrade the floor you will protect the community against hit (if the level of the floor as high as the severity of risk the community will survive even risk occur).

The game also provides the knowledge of reparation for risk will ensure the sustainable development in changing world nowadays. If people have knowledge of risk they will react properly: (i) they will build up a protection system that is strong enough to face to risk; (ii) they will establish an early alarming system in order to deal with risk. Depending on the alarming they will adjust their behavior with the level of risk; (iii) an community connections is need to be create to share the knowledge, damage.

When I played the game only once, I decide base on my instinct and theory that if I buy insurance I will have opportunity to win the game because insurance company will pay for my damages. When I play game many times and after upgrade level I recognize that If want to become winner I have to change my idea on insurance and I have tactics as well as strategy to play game: (i) I have to use high speed processing and touch-screen computer to speed up the money I gain from game: (ii) I have to upgrade spots to make them as strong as they can to protect themselves and upgrade the floor to protect community; (iii) I have to do research enough time to have more time in connecting communities; (iv) I have connecting communities to share damages.

 

The game is very interesting and complicated. But I thing there is no perfect things.You can change: (i) the kind of risk like storm, volcano… instead of comics hit the earth; (ii) the directions of comic’s occurrence instead of occurrence from the left; (iii) accept the continuous level instead of start at the beginning.
