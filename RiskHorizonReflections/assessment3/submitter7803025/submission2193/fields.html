Risk management plays a vital role for every action whether from individuals, community or state. A horizon should always be maintained towards monitoring risk. The role of risk management in the game is to apply and implement the idea and concept of confronting the risk, preparing for it and to cope with the effects. The choices to be made range on the focus of development and allocating resources among the development plan, obtaining knowledge, insurance and protection as well as the preparation to cope up once the damages occur. Risk management offers trade-off between risk and return. Given the scarce resources, wherein every action entails cost, I have to distribute well the resources among the cost development goals, obtaining knowledge, protection and insurance and a contingency fund to cope up with damages. Risk management helps in assessing actions to achieve the goal of increasing the benefits as well as decreasing the losses. The preparation for risk affects development through the growth brought about by the development. The positive growth may be sustained even there are negative shocks when there are enough protection to mitigate risk. It may also slash or delay its growth when there is no enough preparation for risk and/or when the preparation for risk does not meet the loss brought about by the shocks. Preparation for risk may affect development immediately because of the protection enveloping development although the effect and assessment of preparation would materialize when the actual shocks occur-depending on the effect of this shock to the development.

I played the game several times. For the first instance, I focus on development (building all the pods), taking for granted the need to spend resources on knowledge, protection and insurance. Moreover, I set aside the possibility of loss upholding my high appetite for risk even without better preparation. I disregard the concept of balance among these aspects-development, knowledge, protection and insurance as well as managing damages after the shocks happened. With my succeeding attempts, I spend my resources on obtaining the maximum protection and insurance. Therefore, losing the game. After several attempts, I learned that development may not need to be as fast as I want to or as protected as I want to. Perhaps, development may be progressive while maintaining on a proactive risk management. Development needs to be back-up by protection and insurance as well as investing on research to acquire information to mitigate the loss and to achieve balance between progressive development and regressive loss brought about by shocks. Furthermore, resources should always be sufficient to prepare for the unforeseen damages after the shocks to immediately repair the damages and rebuild the affected community.Risk Preparation needs to be timely and relevant as shocks and possible loss may happen anytime of the day.

The main goal of this is to immediately respond and manage the disaster to reduce the number of casualties affected by the aftershocks incident of the primary disaster. For example, a response to reduce the death tolls out of hunger and crimes as a result of the destructed community due to typhoon.
