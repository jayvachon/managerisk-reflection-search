Research,protection,insurance and building were 4 ways to cope with the collision risk in the game.When I started the game, first I built as more constructions as I could, because with more buildings we could connect together and made the insurance effective.Second, I kept raising the lever of protection before the comets came, which could reduce the damage while collision happened. Third, insurance was so important and I got that before we researched.Finally, I started our research as soon as comets emerged, the more research we did, the safer we were, even with a star whose severity or chance was small, we believed sufficient preparation could promise our safe. The truth is I successfully protect my territory after almost ten times’ trying, which means every collision made no damage, so I felt confused why I always stuck in Lever 2, I guess maybe the lever of my constructions was too low.

 

During the whole game, time and the growth of money were the biggest obstacle for me, even though I know how important to develop our building, I went out of money very time. In this case, financing and resourses had to speed up with risk management.

 

Even so, the power of risk management had make an deeply impression to me, no matter how severe the collision is, we still can protect and save our home from damage. I have to say, we had to keep risk management in mind every step. Because if you were damaged in the first beginning, you will fall behind and it’s hard to catch up. If you made your losses when your cities are developed, your loss will be higher, and you had to spend more money to reconstruction.

 

For me, the comets’ collision represented all kinds of risk in our real world, no matter how many more ways or more means to manage there will be, if we could use it right and use it  in time strategically, we could protect our home effectively.
