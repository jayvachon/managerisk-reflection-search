What was the role of risk management in the game?

What choices did you make and what trade-offs did you confront in terms of knowledge, protection, and In what ways did preparation for risk affect development (immediately, or after some time)? Insurance?

Risk management was necessary in the game to prepare, avoid and to cope the shock from the damage caused by the comets. Risk management played a vital role in the game

I invested in the research, and this gave me an informed knowledge and insight of the impending comets.  Protection also sharpened my judgment and vital decision making.

My best choice was to purchase insurance cover. Even though I had limited funds at my disposal, It was a good investment. The cost of repair and maintenance drastically reduced, and this aided in coping with the shocks. Sometimes almost immediately I am been protected against considerable shocks, while at other time the benefits come later. I also made sure to balance the pressure from the comets, by the use of the impact meter.

I will want to improve upon my playing time of the game. In my first attempt, I realized that, I had unnecessarily spent valuable time. I realized in the game that effective time management is also a considerable factor for development. I paid dearly for it when I procrastinated in my first attempt.

I will also make sure to purchase the insurance cover earlier than before,  since it is better that way  than wait for damage to repair damages by the comets, and that even serves as a drawback and dampens the progress of development.
