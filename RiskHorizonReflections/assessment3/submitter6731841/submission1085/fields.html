What was the role of risk management in the game?

1.       The role of risk management in the game is as a determinant of the allocation of resources, e.g. time and money

2.       It also determines how long the colony exists! In the game, risk management is very key to how much development continues in the colony.

3.       Where risks management is poorly done, not fully assessed and prepared for, all investments of time and resources go to waste.

4.       Risk management also underscores the importance of insurance when investing in development.

What choices did you make and what trade-offs did you confront in terms of knowledge, protection, and insurance?

1.       Firstly I had to trade off the speed/rate of development by allocating large funds to high quality (i.e. expensive) insurance, leaving less funds for the construction of pods. This means that pods were more resistant to meteors, even though the pods were not upgraded as quickly as one would have preferred.

2.       Second, I also focused more resources on upgrading already existing pods as these were more resistant to mentors when compared with newer pods. In effect, these kept my investments around for much longer, especially as meteor attacks became more frequent.

1.       Preparation for risk made developments stronger and longer lasting.

2.       It also preserved/secured the investments made in the growth of the colony.

3.       Preparation for risk management created more time to create other pods (Investments)
