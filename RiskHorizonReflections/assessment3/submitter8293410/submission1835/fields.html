The role of risk management in the game was to reduce the risks caused by the falling comets while trying to enhance development as much as possible within Alora’s community, thus, by trying to balance between growth and preparation for shocks. In terms of knowledge, protection and insurance I tried to balance the three aspects trying to also spend on development of the community although I generally spent more on protection and insurance thinking that if on the one hand protection would avoid damage while if damage occured purchasing insurance would then allow me to spend less to recover from damage. Risk management definitely affected Alora’s development by positively influencing its growth and this was evident right from the beginning. For instance, the fact of successfully managing the shocks caused by the comets would avoid coping costs which could instead be used to further develop pods or to start new ones.

 

If I were to play the game again I would probably spend more money on protection since it seemed to me that I could have further avoided risks related to the falling of comets into the ocean. Also I would probably spend a bit less on insurance and a bit more on knowledge so as to balance the three aspects a bit more and be more prepared.

 

To better represent the aspects of risk management of the place that I live in, I would add a warning system button in the game. This could allow the player to understand the benefits of a simple but effective and efficient mode of protection which is very useful where I come from.
