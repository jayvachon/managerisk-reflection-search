I have played many video games in my life for entertainment purpose after working and studying times since I was a kid. After playing the Risk Horizon game, I realize that this is the most amazing game I have known.

Risk Horizon game is a real time strategy game that teaches how and why risk management is important for healthy development. In the game, we will have to manage a society facing with shocks (comets) over the times. To reach the goal of healthy development of community, we need to balance between growing the society and prepare for the potential shocks. This becomes the role of risk management in the game. During game, we have 3 power abilities to help: Research, Protection and Insurance. With the Research Power, we could learn the chance of comet hitting, the time until impact and the severity of the comet. Protection power improves the protection level in the long term. By purchasing the Insurance Power, it is quicker and cheaper to rebuild the community after the comet shock. But there is a clear trade-off that we should consider. Improving the powers will cost you resources.

I played the game two times. At the first time, I stopped at level 3. I just focused on the development by using all the resources on building community. And then, when a lot of shocks occurred at the same time, I realized that it might be too late to protect or buy insurance. Therefore, I restarted. At the second time, on one hand, I still concentrated on the community development at the beginning, to receive more resources. On the other hand, I developed a good Protection Power. I also purchased the Insurance when there was the high probability of hitting comet occurred. In addition, when protection and insurance could not stop the damage from comets, I improved the Research system to have more time to connect the community, raising protection level. And then, I obtained the level 5. From the game, I knew one important thing that is protection the community is useful, but can get expensive. The higher the community rises, the more expensive it becomes to raise its further. Diminishing return is a fact of life here.

This is an amazing game with great features. Suppose I could have a chance to join in developing a new version of the game, I would improve the speed of wealth rising when we obtain the equal development. When playing, I saw that there was no difference between inequality developments. 9 communities with the identical level (such as level 2) give the same speed with 1 community level 5 and 8 others level 1.
