In the game we had to fill a development bar by building and upgrading pods. Each pod helped to increase a rate of development. A risk in this game are the comets that strike the planet and cause a damage to the pods and hinder the development of the planet. Every comet had a severity and chance of hit. So, I increased protection and only when it was necessary (when there was a large chance for the comet to hit). I played the game once and reached level 7. I would not do anything differently.
