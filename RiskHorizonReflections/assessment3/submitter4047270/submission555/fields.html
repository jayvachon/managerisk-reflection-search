This course has given me an exposure of various types of risks that may affect an individual , community , and society at large, the ways of dealing the risks  through knowledge, insurance , protection before the happening of an event  (disaster) and coping with its effects. It is to be known that risk cannot be eliminated but can be minimized to the extent of the damage that it causes.

Risk management can both increase economic returns and reduce the propensity for crises: there need not be a trade-off between resilience and growth.

Playing this game was fun as it was an perfect example of Risk Management. When I first played level one I built three pods purchased an insurance plan , through protection i raised the level of land, I did a little upgrading of pods  but in this process I did very little research about comets therefore making me unaware of the adversities / severity that the comets may cause and the chances of the comet having a fall and damaging the pods. That cost me dearly. I therefore lost level one as I couldn't make it in time. From this I learnt that more research gives you enough knowledge and that knowledge is an integral part of Risk Management.

So when I made a second attempt I made sure that I don't handle  the game like before. In this game I made two major changes


1.  First of all I ensured that the  area is fully utilised and ensured equal development / upgrade of all pods. I purchased the                highest possible insurance plan and did an equal effort on research i.e knowledge , insurance , and protection so that there is    overall progress keeping in mind the target  i.e. going to the next level.

2. I also ensured that there is sustainable development and that I may not face any shortage of funds because lesser the funds      slower the progress , higher the risk involved. I also kept a continuous check (research) on comets that had high chances of  hitting and the severity.

From this attempt I learnt a lesson that if there is an equal progress with research protection and insurance , the community may face disasters better. A community also helps each other by sharing resources in times of disaster.There was lesser damages. This time as compared to before I made use of a large area rather than developing a small area with a few pods itself . This way I reached the fourth level making me  understand the concepts better.

This process from preparedness to coping is an important part of risk management and will take a long way in dealing with disasters. This way an economy can develop faster and there will be lesser loss of life and property through resilience therefore ensuring prosperity.
