1.       - The role of risk management in the game is how to make a community develop through a lot of risk, made by the comet.

          - During the game, I already chose the functions: Research, Protection and Insurance to finish the tasks.

          - At beginning of the game, I always choose Insurance which can cover 70% to have highest return when accident occurs.

          - When facing with hazard, as a comet coming, I use the Research to know its “severity” & “Chance of hit”.

                 + If the level of “severity” & “Chance of hit” are so high, I immediately take Protection to minimize the damage status if accident happens or make the comet miss my community.

                 + If the level of “severity” & “Chance of hit” are very low, I see that no need to use “Protection” to save money and build more building to earn much more money.

2.       - I played game again and again after the first time, maybe 10 times until I won level 4.

          - In the next times, I focus to control the condition of the game, via Research, I can estimate the damage if it hits my community. If the level of risk is so high, I must use Protection to lift the ground higher to minimize or prevent damage. If the level so low, instead of using Protection, I build or upgrade the existing building to earn more money.

          - The main things I learn in this game are “if you want to develop, you must manage the risk strictly & balance with building an opportunity to grow up!” If you only focus on Protection, you may lose some chances to improve yours, it’s a pity. But if you only have action for development without conducting risk assessment and find out some efficiently way to manage, you would face of a lot of accidents that can destroy all of what you’re trying to do or even that it can make Economic Crisis, claim people life and more than that.

          -The hazard in the game, the coming comet, is still OK but how many time people in this Global must face with it? So in my opinion, we’re better change hazard to others such as storm, flood, drought,… and I think it’s much more realistic.

          - I live in Viet Nam, a tropical climate country, year by year facing with storms, floods so I recommend that add more Protection agencies as dikes, dams to manage hazards to protect people & harvest.
